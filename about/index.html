About AI Guerrilla

AI news and education for operators who build real things

Who Is James?

James is an AI educator, operator, and the founder of the AI Cult Leader brand. He’s not a researcher or an academic — he’s a practitioner who uses AI to build real businesses and teaches others to do the same.

After spending years watching hype cycles come and go, James started AI Guerrilla to cut through the noise: no fluff, no theory, just what actually works for operators building with AI today.

Our Mission

AI Guerrilla exists to make AI accessible, actionable, and profitable for operators and entrepreneurs. We cover AI industry news from the angle that matters: what does this mean for the people building with it?

Every article is an analysis — not a press release reprint. James reads the source, forms an opinion, and tells you what to actually do about it.

Editorial Standards

We report from primary sources: company blogs, official press releases, SEC filings, GitHub releases, and direct announcements. Every claim is sourced. Every analysis is original.

We never publish AI-generated content without human editorial review. James reads and approves every article before it goes live. See our full Editorial Policy.
