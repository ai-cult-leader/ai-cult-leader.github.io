Best AI Music Generation Tools in 2025

By Jesse Hull | AI Guerrilla | Updated March 2026

As a music creator or operator, staying ahead of the curve in AI music generation is crucial for producing high-quality content efficiently. In 2025, several AI music generation tools have emerged, each with its unique features and pricing, making it essential to choose the right one for your needs.

Here are six AI music generation tools to consider:

  • AIVA: AIVA is an AI music composition tool that allows users to create custom music tracks for various applications, including film, video games, and advertisements, with a user-friendly interface and a vast library of sounds. Pricing starts at $29/month for the basic plan, with the premium plan costing $99/month.
  • Amper Music: Amper Music is an AI music composition platform that enables users to create custom music tracks in minutes, with a range of styles and genres to choose from, and integration with popular digital audio workstations. Pricing starts at $0/month for the basic plan, with the premium plan costing $12/month.
  • Jukedeck: Jukedeck is an AI music generation tool that uses machine learning algorithms to create custom music tracks for various applications, including video content, ads, and video games, with a user-friendly interface and a vast library of sounds. Pricing starts at $0.99/track for the basic plan, with the premium plan costing $49/month.
  • Magenta Studio: Magenta Studio is an AI music generation tool developed by Google, which allows users to create custom music tracks using a range of machine learning algorithms and a user-friendly interface. Pricing is free, with optional paid upgrades for additional features.
  • Soundraw: Soundraw is an AI music generation tool that enables users to create custom music tracks for various applications, including video content, ads, and video games, with a user-friendly interface and a vast library of sounds. Pricing starts at $16/month for the basic plan, with the premium plan costing $49/month.
  • Landr: Landr is an AI music generation tool that allows users to create custom music tracks, as well as mastered and distributed music, with a range of machine learning algorithms and a user-friendly interface. Pricing starts at $0/month for the basic plan, with the premium plan costing $13.50/month.

Based on features, pricing, and user reviews, the top three AI music generation tools are AIVA, Amper Music, and Magenta Studio. AIVA stands out for its high-quality sound library and user-friendly interface, while Amper Music offers an unparalleled level of customization and integration with popular digital audio workstations. Magenta Studio, on the other hand, offers a free and highly versatile platform for music creation, making it an ideal choice for beginners and intermediate users.

Here are three use-case scenarios for these tools:

  • Beginner: For a beginner, Magenta Studio is an ideal choice, as it offers a free and user-friendly platform for creating custom music tracks, with a range of machine learning algorithms and a vast library of sounds.
  • Intermediate: For an intermediate user, Amper Music is a great choice, as it offers a range of customization options and integration with popular digital audio workstations, making it ideal for creating high-quality music tracks for video content, ads, and video games.
  • Advanced: For an advanced user, AIVA is the best choice, as it offers a high-quality sound library and a user-friendly interface, making it ideal for creating complex and customized music tracks for film, video games, and advertisements.

To start using AI music generation tools today, follow these action steps:

  1. Sign up for a free trial or basic plan with one of the top three tools (AIVA, Amper Music, or Magenta Studio).
  2. Explore the tool's interface and features, and watch tutorials to get started with creating custom music tracks.
  3. Start creating music tracks using the tool's machine learning algorithms and sound library, and experiment with different customization options and genres.

By following these steps and choosing the right AI music generation tool for your needs, you can create high-quality music tracks efficiently and take your music creation to the next level.
