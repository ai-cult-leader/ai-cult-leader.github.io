AI Music

Best AI Music Generation Tools in 2025

By Jesse Hull | AI Guerrilla | Updated March 2026

As the music industry continues to evolve, AI music generation tools have become an essential asset for operators and creators looking to streamline their workflow and unlock new creative possibilities. In this guide, we'll explore the best AI music generation tools in 2025, highlighting their features, pricing, and use-case scenarios to help you make informed decisions and get started with AI-powered music creation.

Here are six AI music generation tools worth considering:

  • Amper Music: Amper Music is a cloud-based AI music composition platform that allows users to create custom music tracks in minutes, with a vast library of sounds and styles to choose from. Pricing starts at $19/month for the Basic plan, with the Pro plan available for $49/month and the Business plan for $199/month.
  • AIVA: AIVA is an AI-powered music composition platform that uses machine learning algorithms to create original music tracks, with a focus on film and video game soundtracks. Pricing starts at $29/month for the Creator plan, with the Pro plan available for $99/month and the Enterprise plan for custom pricing.
  • Soundtrap: Soundtrap is a cloud-based digital audio workstation (DAW) that incorporates AI-powered music generation tools, allowing users to create and edit music tracks with ease. Pricing starts at $7.99/month for the Basic plan, with the Pro plan available for $13.99/month and the Premium plan for $19.99/month.
  • Magenta Studio: Magenta Studio is a free, open-source AI music generation platform developed by the Google Brain team, offering a range of tools and plugins for music creation and experimentation. Pricing is free, with optional donations to support the development of the platform.
  • LANDR: LANDR is a cloud-based music creation platform that offers AI-powered music generation tools, including beat-making and chord progression generation. Pricing starts at $6.99/month for the Basic plan, with the Pro plan available for $14.99/month and the Enterprise plan for custom pricing.
  • Mubert: Mubert is an AI-powered music generation platform that creates original music tracks based on user input, with a focus on ambient and background music. Pricing starts at $10/month for the Personal plan, with the Business plan available for $50/month and the Enterprise plan for custom pricing.

Based on our analysis, the top three AI music generation tools are Amper Music, AIVA, and Soundtrap, due to their ease of use, versatility, and high-quality output. These tools offer a range of features and pricing plans to suit different needs and budgets, making them ideal for operators and creators looking to integrate AI music generation into their workflow.

Here are three use-case scenarios for AI music generation tools:

  • Beginner: Using Amper Music to create a custom music track for a social media video, with a focus on ease of use and speedy delivery.
  • Intermediate: Using AIVA to generate original music tracks for a video game soundtrack, with a focus on high-quality output and customization options.
  • Advanced: Using Soundtrap to create and edit a complex music track with AI-powered tools, with a focus on advanced features and collaboration capabilities.

To start using AI music generation tools today, follow these action steps:

  1. Sign up for a free trial or basic plan with one of the top three tools (Amper Music, AIVA, or Soundtrap) to explore their features and capabilities.
  2. Watch tutorial videos and online courses to learn the basics of AI music generation and the specific tool you've chosen.
  3. Start creating music tracks using the tool's pre-built templates and AI-powered features, and experiment with different sounds and styles to find what works best for your project.

By following these steps and leveraging the power of AI music generation tools, you can unlock new creative possibilities and streamline your music creation workflow, whether you're a beginner or an advanced operator.
