Profile

Satya Nadella is the CEO of Microsoft who made the boldest AI bet in corporate history—investing $13 billion in OpenAI. Under Nadella, Microsoft integrated GPT-4 into Copilot across the entire Microsoft 365 suite, bringing AI to hundreds of millions of enterprise users.

His vision of AI as a co-pilot for every human activity has reshaped Microsoft from a declining software giant into the most valuable AI company in the world. Also brought Mustafa Suleyman from DeepMind to lead Microsoft AI.
