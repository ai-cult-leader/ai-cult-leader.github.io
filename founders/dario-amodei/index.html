Profile

Dario Amodei is the CEO and co-founder of Anthropic, the AI safety company behind Claude. Previously VP of Research at OpenAI, he left with his sister Daniela and other colleagues to found Anthropic with a focus on AI safety research.

Dario pioneered Constitutional AI and RLHF techniques that now underpin Claude. Known for thoughtful long-form essays on AI risk, his vision for responsible AI development, and building one of the most technically impressive AI labs in the world.
