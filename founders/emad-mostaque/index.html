Profile

Emad Mostaque founded Stability AI and released Stable Diffusion as open source, which democratized AI image generation and sparked the entire open-source AI art movement. Under his leadership, Stability AI became one of the highest-valued AI startups before turbulent governance issues led to his departure in 2024.

Post-Stability, he has continued advocating for decentralized AI and sovereign AI infrastructure. The release of Stable Diffusion changed the AI landscape permanently by proving that frontier image models could be open-sourced.
