Profile

Pieter Abbeel is a leading AI robotics researcher and co-founder of Covariant, which builds AI brains for industrial robots. A UC Berkeley professor and former research scientist at OpenAI, Abbeel pioneered Apprenticeship Learning and has trained thousands of AI researchers.

Covariant uses large-scale reinforcement learning to create robot AI that generalizes across tasks—solving one of manufacturing's core automation challenges. His research spans robot learning, imitation learning, and AI for physical systems.
