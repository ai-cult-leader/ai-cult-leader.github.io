Profile

Guillaume Lample is a co-founder and Chief Scientist at Mistral AI, responsible for the technical direction of their model development. Previously a research scientist at Meta AI, he was instrumental in developing LLaMA and other foundational models.

At Mistral, Lample led the technical work on Mistral 7B—the model that proved small models could outperform much larger ones. His research expertise spans NLP, mathematical reasoning, and efficient model architectures.
