Profile

Aravind Srinivas co-founded Perplexity AI, reimagining search for the AI era. Previously a researcher at OpenAI and DeepMind, he identified a fundamental gap in AI: the need for grounded, cited AI answers rather than hallucinated responses.

Perplexity now processes hundreds of millions of queries monthly and is backed by Jeff Bezos, NVIDIA, and Sequoia. Srinivas' vision of AI that cites sources has influenced how the industry thinks about trustworthy AI responses and the future of web search.
