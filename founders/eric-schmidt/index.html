Profile

Eric Schmidt served as Google CEO from 2001-2011 and has remained one of the most active voices in AI policy and national security technology. Chairs the Special Competitive Studies Project focused on US technological competitiveness with China.

His book "The Age of AI" with Henry Kissinger analyzes geopolitical implications of AI. Advises Congress, the Department of Defense, and the National Security Commission on AI. Schmidt's combination of technical depth and policy influence makes him uniquely positioned in AI governance discussions.
