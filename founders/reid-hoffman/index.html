Profile

Reid Hoffman co-founded LinkedIn and has been one of the most active AI investors and thinkers. As a Greylock partner, he's backed AI companies including OpenAI. Co-founded Inflection AI with Mustafa Suleyman.

His book "Impromptu" with GPT-4 explored AI's potential for human collaboration. Hoffman advocates for AI as a tool for human flourishing and has been vocal about AI regulation. His networks bridge Silicon Valley, policy makers, and the global business community, making him uniquely influential in shaping AI's social context.
