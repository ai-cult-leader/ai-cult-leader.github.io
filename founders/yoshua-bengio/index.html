Profile

Yoshua Bengio is a Turing Award winner and co-founder of Mila, the Quebec AI Institute. A foundational researcher in deep learning who pioneered attention mechanisms, recurrent networks, and generative models that power today's AI.

Unlike colleagues who moved to industry, Bengio remains in academia advocating for open AI research and rigorous safety work. He has become increasingly vocal about AI existential risk and signed letters calling for AI development pauses. His research group continues producing influential work on AI safety and interpretability.
