Profile

Yann LeCun is Chief AI Scientist at Meta and a Turing Award winner credited with inventing convolutional neural networks (CNNs), the foundation of modern computer vision. A vocal critic of large language models and current AI hype, LeCun argues that true AI intelligence requires a different architecture—world models—not next-token prediction.

His JEPA (Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture) research aims to create AI that understands the physical world. His contrarian views and prolific Twitter presence make him one of the most influential voices in AI research.
