Profile

Sam Altman is the CEO of OpenAI, the company that created ChatGPT, GPT-4, and is leading the race to artificial general intelligence. Before OpenAI, Altman was President of Y Combinator, the legendary startup accelerator.

Under his leadership, OpenAI launched ChatGPT in November 2022, which reached 100 million users in 2 months—the fastest consumer product adoption in history. Known for his bold predictions about AGI timelines and his role in shaping AI policy discussions worldwide.
