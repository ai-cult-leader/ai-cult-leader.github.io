Best AI Image Generation Tools in 2025

By Jesse Hull | AI Guerrilla | Updated March 2026

As the demand for high-quality visual content continues to grow, AI image generation tools have become an essential asset for operators and creators, enabling them to produce stunning images with minimal effort and expertise. In 2025, the market is flooded with AI image generation tools, but it's crucial to choose the right one that fits your needs and budget, which is why we've compiled a list of the best tools available.

Here are six specific AI image generation tools, each with its unique features and pricing:

  • Deep Dream Generator: This tool uses a neural network to generate surreal and dreamlike images from any given picture, with pricing starting at $9.99/month for the basic plan and $29.99/month for the premium plan. Deep Dream Generator offers a unique and creative way to generate images, but its limited customization options may not appeal to all users.
  • Prisma: Prisma uses AI to transform your photos into works of art in the style of famous artists such as Van Gogh and Picasso, with pricing starting at $7.99/month for the basic plan and $14.99/month for the premium plan. Prisma is a great tool for those who want to add a touch of creativity to their images, but its limited output resolution may not be suitable for commercial use.
  • DALL-E: DALL-E is a powerful AI image generation tool that can create realistic images from text prompts, with pricing starting at $30/month for the basic plan and $100/month for the premium plan. DALL-E offers unparalleled customization options and image quality, making it a top choice for professionals and businesses.
  • Midjourney: Midjourney is an AI-powered image generation tool that can create stunning images from text prompts, with pricing starting at $10/month for the basic plan and $30/month for the premium plan. Midjourney offers a great balance between image quality and affordability, making it a popular choice among creators and operators.
  • Artbreeder: Artbreeder is a unique AI image generation tool that allows users to create and evolve original artwork, with pricing starting at $9.99/month for the basic plan and $24.99/month for the premium plan. Artbreeder offers a unique and interactive way to generate images, but its limited output resolution may not be suitable for commercial use.
  • Stable Diffusion: Stable Diffusion is an open-source AI image generation tool that can create high-quality images from text prompts, with pricing starting at $0 (free) for the basic plan and $20/month for the premium plan. Stable Diffusion offers unparalleled flexibility and customization options, making it a top choice for developers and power users.

Based on our analysis, the top three AI image generation tools are DALL-E, Midjourney, and Stable Diffusion, due to their exceptional image quality, customization options, and affordability. These tools offer a great balance between creative freedom and commercial viability, making them ideal for operators and creators who need high-quality images for various use cases.

Here are three use-case scenarios for AI image generation tools:

  • Beginner: A social media manager wants to create engaging visual content for their brand's Instagram page, using an AI image generation tool to create eye-catching images from text prompts. With Midjourney, they can create high-quality images for $10/month, without requiring extensive design expertise.
  • Intermediate: A marketing agency wants to create custom images for their client's advertising campaign, using an AI image generation tool to generate realistic images from text prompts. With DALL-E, they can create professional-grade images for $30/month, with unparalleled customization options and image quality.
  • Advanced: A developer wants to integrate AI image generation capabilities into their web application, using an open-source AI image generation tool to create custom images on the fly. With Stable Diffusion, they can access high-quality image generation capabilities for free, with the option to upgrade to a premium plan for $20/month.

To start using AI image generation tools today, follow these action steps:

  1. Sign up for a free trial or basic plan with one of the top three tools (DALL-E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion) to test their capabilities and pricing.
  2. Experiment with different text prompts and customization options to get a feel for the tool's capabilities and limitations.
  3. Upgrade to a premium plan or explore additional features
