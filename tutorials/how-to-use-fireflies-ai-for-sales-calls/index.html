How to Use Fireflies.ai for Sales Calls

How to use Fireflies.ai for Sales Calls

As a sales professional, you know the importance of effective call recording and note-taking. Fireflies.ai is an AI-powered tool that can help you streamline your sales calls and boost productivity. The good news is that Fireflies.ai offers a free tier, so you can get started right away.

What you need:

To get started with Fireflies.ai, you'll need a computer with a reliable internet connection, and a Fireflies.ai account (which can be set up for free). You'll also need a phone or computer with a microphone to record your calls.

Step 1: Getting started with Fireflies.ai

  1. Create a Fireflies.ai account by going to www.fireflies.ai and signing up.
  2. Download and install the Fireflies.ai browser extensions (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) or desktop app.
  3. Configure your account settings, such as your email address and password.

Step 2: Setting up for Sales Calls

  1. Launch Fireflies.ai and click on the "Add Call" button.
  2. Select the type of call you're about to make (e.g., sales call, customer support call, etc.).
  3. Choose the phone number or dialer you want to use to make the call.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Make the sales call as you normally would.
  2. Fireflies.ai will automatically record the call and transcribe it in real-time.
  3. As you talk, Fireflies.ai will create a note-taking interface where you can jot down important details, such as customer concerns, next steps, and follow-up tasks.
  4. After the call, review the recording and notes, and update your CRM with the necessary information.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid:

Make sure to review and update your CRM after each call. This will help you stay organized and ensure that you don't miss any important follow-up tasks.
