How to Use Pictory for Video Summaries

How to use Pictory for Video Summaries

Pictory is a powerful AI-powered video creation tool that allows you to create engaging video summaries from long-form content. The good news is that Pictory offers a free tier, so you can start using it right away.

Step 1: Getting started with Pictory

To get started with Pictory, go to their website and sign up for a free account. You'll need to provide some basic information, such as your name and email address.

Step 2: Setting up for Video Summaries

Once you've signed up, click on the "Create a new project" button and select the "Video Summary" template. You'll then need to upload the long-form content you want to summarize - this could be a video, article, or even a podcast.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Choose the type of summary you want to create - Pictory offers a range of templates, from 1-minute summaries to 60-second highlights.
  2. Select the style of the summary - you can choose from a range of templates, including animated text, images, and more.
  3. Customize the summary by adding your own images, text overlays, and more.
  4. Review and fine-tune your summary - you can make changes to the text, images, and even the music.
  5. Export your summary in the desired format - Pictory supports a range of formats, including MP4, GIF, and more.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One common mistake to avoid is trying to cram too much information into your summary. Remember, the goal of a video summary is to give your audience a quick overview of the main points, not to provide a detailed analysis.
