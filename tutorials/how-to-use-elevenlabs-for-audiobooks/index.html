How to Use ElevenLabs for Audiobooks

How to use ElevenLabs for Audiobooks

As a creator who uses ElevenLabs daily, I'm excited to share my step-by-step guide on how to use this powerful tool for creating amazing audiobooks.

What you need

To get started, you'll need an ElevenLabs account, either on the free tier or one of their paid plans. The free tier is a great starting point, allowing you to create up to 10 hours of audio per month.

Step 1: Getting started with ElevenLabs

First, head over to the ElevenLabs website and sign up for an account. Once you've verified your email address, log in and navigate to the dashboard.

Step 2: Setting up for Audiobooks

Now, click on the "Projects" tab and create a new project for your audiobook. You can choose from a variety of settings, such as voice, tempo, and language. For audiobooks, I recommend using the "Voice-Over" setting and selecting a high-quality voice that suits your story.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Upload your text file or paste it into the input field. ElevenLabs supports a variety of file formats, including TXT, PDF, and DOCX.
  2. Choose your desired voice and settings from the dropdown menus.
  3. Click the "Create Audio" button and wait for the magic to happen. Depending on the length of your text and the complexity of your settings, this might take a few minutes or a few hours.
  4. Once the audio is generated, click on the "Download" button to save your audiobook as an MP3 file.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One common mistake to avoid is over-optimizing your settings. While it's tempting to experiment with different voices and tempo settings, remember that the goal is to create a high-quality audiobook that complements your story. Stick to the basics and adjust as needed.
