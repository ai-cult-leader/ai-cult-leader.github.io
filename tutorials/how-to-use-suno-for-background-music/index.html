How to Use Suno for Background Music

How to use Suno for Background Music

What you need

Suno is a user-friendly AI-powered music generator that provides a free tier with some limitations. You can sign up and start creating music right away, but keep in mind that the free tier has a 3-song limit per day.

Step 1: Getting started with Suno

To get started with Suno, go to suno.ai and sign up with your email or Google account. You'll receive a verification email, and once you've verified your email, you can log in to your account.

Step 2: Setting up for Background Music

After logging in, click on the 'Create Music' button. You'll be prompted to choose a music genre, mood, and tempo. For background music, I recommend choosing a mellow genre like 'Lo-Fi' or 'Electronic' and a calm mood like 'Relaxing' or 'Focus.'

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Click on the 'Generate' button to create your music.
  2. Once your music is generated, you can save it to your Suno library or export it as an MP3 file.
  3. To customize your music further, click on the 'Edit' button and adjust the parameters like tempo, pitch, and instrumentation.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One common mistake to avoid is over-editing your music. Remember that Suno's AI algorithm has already generated a great track, so focus on fine-tuning it instead of drastically changing its core elements.
