How to Use Suno for Music Production

How to use Suno for Music Production

Suno is a powerful AI music production tool that offers a free tier, making it accessible to creators of all levels. To get started, you'll need a computer with a stable internet connection and a DAW (digital audio workstation) of your choice.

Step 1: Getting started with Suno

Visit the Suno website and sign up for an account. You can choose to start with the free tier, which offers a generous amount of credits and access to most features.

  1. Click on the "Sign up" button and enter your email address and password.
  2. Verify your email address by clicking on the link sent to you by Suno.
  3. Login to your account and start exploring the platform.

Step 2: Setting up for Music Production

To set up Suno for music production, you'll need to create a new project and choose the right settings. Follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "New Project" button and choose the type of music you want to produce (e.g. electronic, hip-hop, etc.).
  2. Select the tempo, time signature, and key of your project.
  3. Choose the desired resolution and sample rate for your project.

Step 3: The actual workflow

Now it's time to start creating music with Suno. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Click on the "Generate" button and choose the type of music you want to generate (e.g. melody, chord progression, etc.).
  2. Adjust the settings to your liking and click "Generate" again.
  3. Use the generated music as a starting point and start editing and arranging it to your taste.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One common mistake to avoid is over-relying on Suno's generated music. Remember that music production is a creative process, and it's essential to add your own flair and personality to the generated music.
