How to Use InVideo AI for Youtube Automation

How to use InVideo AI for YouTube Automation

In this tutorial, we'll walk you through the process of setting up and using InVideo AI for automating your YouTube content creation. The good news is that InVideo AI offers a free tier, so you can start experimenting without breaking the bank.

What you need:

InVideo AI account (sign up for free), a computer or mobile device, and a YouTube account.

Step 1: Getting started with InVideo AI

Head over to InVideo AI's website and sign up for a free account. Once you're logged in, you'll see a dashboard with various templates and tools to get you started.

Step 2: Setting up for YouTube Automation

From the dashboard, go to the "Connect" tab and select "YouTube" as the platform you want to connect. Follow the prompts to authenticate your YouTube account.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Choose a template or start from scratch. InVideo AI has a vast library of templates and assets to help you get started.
  2. Click on the "AI" button and select the type of content you want to create (e.g., video, animation, etc.). InVideo AI's AI will start generating content based on your inputs.
  3. Review and edit the generated content as needed. You can add your own voiceovers, music, and effects to make it more engaging.
  4. Export your final video and upload it to YouTube using the InVideo AI "Publish" feature.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

Make sure to review the generated content carefully and edit it as needed. InVideo AI's AI is powerful, but it's not perfect, and you may need to make some adjustments to achieve the desired result.
