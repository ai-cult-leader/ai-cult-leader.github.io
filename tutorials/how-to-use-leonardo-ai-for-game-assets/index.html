How to Use Leonardo AI for Game Assets

How to use Leonardo AI for Game Assets

As a game developer, I've found Leonardo AI to be an indispensable tool for creating high-quality assets quickly and efficiently. With its user-friendly interface and powerful capabilities, it's no wonder why it's become a go-to solution for many game developers.

Please note that Leonardo AI offers a free tier, making it accessible to developers of all levels and budgets.

Step 1: Getting started with Leonardo AI

  1. Head over to the Leonardo AI website and sign up for an account.
  2. Once you've created an account, click on the "Get Started" button to begin exploring the platform.
  3. Take a few minutes to familiarize yourself with the interface and explore the different features and tools available.

Step 2: Setting up for Game Assets

  1. Click on the "New Project" button and select the type of asset you want to create (e.g. 3D model, texture, etc.).
  2. Choose the desired settings and resolution for your asset.
  3. Upload any reference images or 3D models you want to use as a starting point for your asset.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Click on the "Generate" button to start the AI-powered asset creation process.
  2. Once the generation process is complete, review and refine your asset as needed.
  3. Export your final asset in the desired format (e.g. OBJ, PNG, etc.).

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

Don't be afraid to experiment and try out different settings and configurations to achieve the desired look and feel for your asset. Additionally, be mindful of the AI's limitations and don't rely solely on it for critical asset creation – always review and refine your assets carefully.
