How to Use Perplexity for Competitor Research

How to use Perplexity for Competitor Research

Perplexity is a powerful web scraping and competitor research tool that can help you gain valuable insights into your competitors' strategies. The best part? Perplexity offers a free tier, allowing you to start exploring its features without breaking the bank.

Step 1: Getting started with Perplexity

Sign up for a Perplexity account and create a new project. Choose a name for your project and select the 'Create Project' button. This will take you to the project dashboard, where you can start setting up your competitor research.

Step 2: Setting up for Competitor Research

Click on the 'Scrape' tab and enter the website of your competitor. You can also add multiple domains to scrape by using the 'Add Domain' feature. Next, select the specific pages you want to scrape, such as their product pages or blog posts.

Select the data points you want to extract, such as product descriptions, prices, or keywords. You can also use Perplexity's built-in filters to narrow down the data to what's most relevant to your research.

Step 3: The actual workflow

Once you've set up your scrape, click the 'Run' button to start the data extraction process. Perplexity will then provide you with a dataset of extracted data, which you can view in the 'Data' tab.

Use the 'Data' tab to filter, sort, and analyze the extracted data. You can also export the data to CSV or Excel for further analysis.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid:

Make sure to set up your filters carefully to avoid extracting irrelevant data. Also, be mindful of the website's terms of use and scrape responsibly to avoid any issues.
