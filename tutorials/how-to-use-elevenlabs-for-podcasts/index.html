How to Use ElevenLabs for Podcasts

Using ElevenLabs for Podcasts: A Step-by-Step Guide

As a podcaster, I'm excited to share my experience with ElevenLabs, a powerful tool for AI-generated audio content. The good news is that ElevenLabs offers a free tier, perfect for testing the waters and getting a feel for the platform.

What you need:

  • An ElevenLabs account (free tier available)
  • A computer or mobile device with internet access

Step 1: Getting started with ElevenLabs

  1. Head over to ElevenLabs.io and sign up for an account.
  2. Complete the onboarding process, which includes verifying your email address and creating a password.

Step 2: Setting up for Podcasts

  1. Navigate to the "Content" tab and select "Podcast" as the content type.
  2. Choose your desired output format (e.g., MP3, WAV) and quality settings.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Enter your podcast script or provide a link to a Google Doc containing your script.
  2. Select your desired voice, tone, and language settings.
  3. Click "Generate Audio" and wait for the AI to create your podcast episode.
  4. Download your generated audio file and upload it to your podcast hosting platform.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid:

Be mindful of your script's complexity and ensure it's well-structured for optimal AI processing. Avoid using jargon or overly technical terms that may confuse the AI.
