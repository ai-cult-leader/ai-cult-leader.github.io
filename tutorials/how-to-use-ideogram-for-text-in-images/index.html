How to Use Ideogram for Text In Images

How to use Ideogram for Text In Images

Ideogram is my go-to tool for adding text to images, and I'm excited to share my step-by-step guide on how to use it. The good news is that Ideogram offers a free tier, so you can get started right away!

What you need

To get started, all you need is a computer with a stable internet connection and a web browser. You can access Ideogram directly from your browser, so no downloads or installations required.

Step 1: Getting started with Ideogram

  1. Head over to Ideogram's website and click on the "Get Started" button.
  2. You'll be prompted to create an account or log in if you already have one.
  3. Once you're logged in, you'll be taken to the dashboard where you can start creating your first project.

Step 2: Setting up for Text In Images

  1. Click on the "Create New Project" button and select the image you want to add text to.
  2. Choose a text style and font that fits your design aesthetic.
  3. Adjust the text color, size, and position to your liking.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Position your cursor where you want the text to appear and click.
  2. Start typing your text, and Ideogram will automatically adjust the font size and style to fit the image.
  3. Make any final adjustments to the text, and you're done!

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One common mistake to avoid is selecting a font that's too large or too small for the image. Ideogram has a built-in preview feature, so make sure to check how the text will look before finalizing your design.
