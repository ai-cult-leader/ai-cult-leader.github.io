How to Use Synthesia for Explainer Videos

How to Use Synthesia for Explainer Videos

If you're a creator looking to produce high-quality explainer videos quickly and efficiently, Synthesia is a powerful tool to consider. The good news is that Synthesia offers a free tier, allowing you to experiment with the platform and create your first videos without committing to a paid plan.

Step 1: Getting Started with Synthesia

To get started with Synthesia, sign up for an account on their website. The sign-up process is straightforward and should only take a few minutes. Once you've created your account, you'll be presented with a dashboard where you can start exploring the platform's features.

Step 2: Setting Up for Explainer Videos

Before you start creating your explainer video, you'll need to set up a few things. First, create a new project by clicking on the "New Project" button. Then, choose the language and accent of your character. For explainer videos, a neutral accent is usually a safe choice.

Step 3: The Actual Workflow

  1. Create your script: Write a concise and engaging script for your explainer video.

  2. Record your audio: Record your script as an audio file. You can use a microphone or record your audio directly within Synthesia.

  3. Upload your assets: Upload any images or videos you want to use in your explainer video.

  4. Sync your audio and assets: Use Synthesia's editor to sync your audio with your assets.

  5. Rendervideo: Once you've synced your audio and assets, click on the "Render" button to create your explainer video.

Pro Tip or Common Mistake to Avoid

One common mistake to avoid is not leaving enough time for rendering. Synthesia's rendering process can take several minutes, depending on the complexity of your video. Make sure to plan ahead and leave time for rendering before your deadline.
