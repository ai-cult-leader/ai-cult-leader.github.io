How to Use Fathom for Zoom Summaries

How to use Fathom for Zoom Summaries

Fathom is a powerful tool for automating meeting summaries, and it's free to try on their website, including their free tier. This tutorial will walk you through the step-by-step process of setting up Fathom for Zoom summaries.

Step 1: Getting started with Fathom

First, sign up for a Fathom account on their website. You'll need to create an account and verify your email address.

Step 2: Setting up for Zoom Summaries

After verifying your email address, navigate to the Zoom integration page in your Fathom dashboard and click on the "Connect" button. You'll be redirected to the Zoom login page, where you'll need to authenticate your account.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Once you've connected your Zoom account, you'll be taken back to the Fathom dashboard. Click on the "New Meeting" button to create a new summary.
  2. In the meeting summary settings, select the meeting you want to summarize and choose the format you prefer (text, audio, or video).
  3. Click on the "Summary" button to generate the summary. Fathom will automatically transcribe the meeting and summarize it for you.
  4. Review the summary and make any necessary edits. You can also share the summary with others or save it to your Fathom dashboard for future reference.

That's it! With Fathom, you can now automate your Zoom summaries and save time.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

Make sure to test your summary settings before relying on them for important meetings. This will help you catch any errors or issues with the summary generation process.
