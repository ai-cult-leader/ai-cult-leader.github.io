How to Use Runway for Green Screen

How to use Runway for Green Screen

Runway is a powerful AI platform that offers a free tier for individuals and hobbyists. You can get started with the free plan, which includes access to most features.

Step 1: Getting started with Runway

First, sign up for a Runway account. Go to runwayml.com and click on "Sign Up". Fill out the form with your email address, choose a password, and confirm your account via email.

Step 2: Setting up for Green Screen

Once you're logged in, create a new project by clicking on the "+" button in the top-right corner. Choose "Video" as the project type and then click on "Import Video". Select your video file and click on "Next". Now, you'll need to add a green screen track to your project. Click on the "Add Track" button and select "Green Screen" as the track type.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Select your green screen video as the green screen track. You can use the "Browse" button to select a different file.
  2. Adjust the green screen threshold using the slider. This will help Runway detect the green color and replace it with the background image.
  3. Upload your background image by clicking on the "Upload Background Image" button. You can use a high-resolution image or a simple color.
  4. Click on the "Generate" button to start the green screen removal process. This may take a few minutes depending on the size of your video and the complexity of the green screen.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One common mistake to avoid is not adjusting the green screen threshold correctly. If the threshold is too high, Runway may not detect the green color correctly, resulting in a poor green screen removal. On the other hand, if the threshold is too low, Runway may detect other colors as green, resulting in an incorrect green screen removal.
