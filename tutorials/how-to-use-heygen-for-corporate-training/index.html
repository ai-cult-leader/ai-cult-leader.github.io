How to Use HeyGen for Corporate Training

How to use HeyGen for Corporate Training

Using HeyGen for corporate training is a game-changer, and I'm excited to walk you through it.

What you need

You'll need a HeyGen account, which offers a free tier that's perfect for small teams or pilots. You can upgrade to a paid plan as your company grows.

Step 1: Getting started with HeyGen

Head to heygen.com and sign up for an account. Choose the free tier or upgrade to a paid plan depending on your needs.

Step 2: Setting up for Corporate Training

  1. Connect your desired e-learning platforms, such as Google Drive or Slack, to import your content.
  2. Create a new HeyGen project for your corporate training program.
  3. Add your team members and assign roles, such as editors or viewers.

Step 3: The actual workflow

  1. Import your e-learning content into HeyGen, such as videos, presentations, or PDFs.
  2. Use HeyGen's AI-powered editor to create engaging, interactive content, such as quizzes, surveys, or gamification elements.
  3. Distribute your corporate training content to your team members through HeyGen's platform.

Pro tip: Test your content with a small group before rolling it out to the entire team to ensure it meets your needs.

Common mistake to avoid: Don't overdo it with too many features at once. Start simple and iterate based on your team's feedback.
