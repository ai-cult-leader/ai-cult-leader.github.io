How to Use Play.ht for Text To Speech

How to use Play.ht for Text To Speech

### What you need To get started with Play.ht, you'll need a Play.ht account. The good news is that they offer a free tier, which includes 1 million characters per month - perfect for personal projects or testing your workflow. ### Step 1: Getting started with Play.ht

Sign up for a Play.ht account by visiting their website. Fill out the registration form with your email address and password. Once you've completed the registration process, you'll receive an email to verify your account.

### Step 2: Setting up for Text To Speech

After verifying your account, navigate to the dashboard and click on 'Create New Project'. In this step, you'll choose the Text To Speech settings, such as the voice, speed, and pitch. You can select one of the many voices available in the dropdown menu or upload your own custom voice.

### Step 3: The actual workflow
  1. Copy and paste the text you want to convert into speech into the text box. You can also upload a text file or paste a link to a Google Doc.
  2. Choose the desired audio format and quality. You can select from MP3, WAV, or M4A formats in various quality settings.
  3. Click the 'Convert' button to start the Text To Speech process. This may take a few seconds depending on the length of the text.
  4. Once the conversion is complete, you can download the audio file directly from your Play.ht dashboard.
### Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

Be mindful of the character limit on the free tier (1 million characters per month). If you exceed this limit, you'll need to upgrade to a paid plan. To avoid this, consider breaking up long texts into smaller chunks or using a paid plan for high-volume usage.
