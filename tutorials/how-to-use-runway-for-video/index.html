How to Use Runway for Video

How to use Runway for Video

Runway is a powerful AI video editing tool that can help you create stunning visuals in minutes. The good news is that it offers a free tier, so you can start experimenting without breaking the bank.

What you need

To get started, you'll need a laptop or desktop with a decent graphics card, a internet connection, and a Runway account (either free or paid). You can sign up for a free account on the Runway website.

Step 1: Getting started with Runway

  1. Head to the Runway website and sign up for a new account.
  2. Verify your email address and log in to the platform.
  3. Take a few minutes to explore the interface and get familiar with the different tools and features.

Step 2: Setting up for Video

Before you start editing, make sure you have the necessary settings enabled. Go to your account settings and ensure that:

  • Video export is enabled.
  • Your preferred video resolution and frame rate are set.
  • Color management is set to your desired format (e.g. ProRes, H.264, etc).

Step 3: The actual workflow

Now it's time to start editing! Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a stunning video:

  1. Create a new project by clicking on the "+" button in the top-right corner of the screen.
  2. Select your footage and import it into Runway.
  3. Apply a style or effect to your footage using the "Apply" tab on the left-hand side of the screen.
  4. Use the "Adjust" tab to fine-tune your footage and make adjustments as needed.
  5. Export your final video by clicking on the "Export" button in the top-right corner of the screen.

Pro tip or common mistake to avoid

One of the most common mistakes I see creators make is over-relying on autopilot settings. While Runway's AI is incredibly powerful, it's still a machine – and it can make mistakes. Always take a close look at your footage and make adjustments as needed to achieve the look and feel you want.
