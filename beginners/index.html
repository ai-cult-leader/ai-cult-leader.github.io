Questions People Ask Before Getting Started

Is AI safe to use?
Yes! The main AI tools — like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Siri — are made by large, reputable companies and are completely safe to use for asking questions and getting help. As with anything online, you should avoid sharing private information like passwords or your Social Security number. But asking AI "what should I have for dinner?" or "help me write a birthday card" is totally safe.
Do I need to pay for AI?
No! The most popular AI tools are free to use. ChatGPT has a free version at chat.openai.com. Google's Gemini is free. Microsoft Copilot is free. You can do a lot without ever paying anything. Some tools offer paid upgrades with extra features, but the free versions are excellent for everyday use.
Will AI steal my personal information?
AI tools from reputable companies (OpenAI, Google, Microsoft) do not steal your information. However, you should treat AI like you treat any website: don't share your passwords, Social Security number, bank account numbers, or other sensitive private details. Use AI for questions and tasks, not for storing private information.
Do I need a fast computer or special equipment?
Not at all! AI tools work on any computer, tablet, or smartphone made in the last 10 years. If you can open a web browser (like Google Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) and go to a website, you can use AI. No special equipment needed.
What if I make a mistake?
You can't break it! AI is very forgiving. If you type something wrong, just correct it. If the answer isn't what you wanted, ask again in a different way. You can start a completely new conversation at any time. There's no wrong way to try.
Is using AI legal?
Absolutely. Using AI tools like ChatGPT is completely legal for personal use — asking questions, writing letters, getting recipes, learning new things. The same way it's legal to search Google, it's legal to ask an AI.
Will AI replace my job?
AI is a tool that helps people do their jobs better — similar to how computers and the internet changed work but didn't eliminate it. If you're retired, this isn't something to worry about. If you're still working, learning to use AI is actually a great way to make yourself more valuable at your job.
How do I get started today?
Right here! Start with our "What is AI?" guide — it takes about 5 minutes to read and will answer all your questions. Then try the ChatGPT beginner guide and have your first AI conversation. You'll be amazed how easy it actually is.
