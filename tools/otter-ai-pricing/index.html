Otter.ai Pricing 2026

Otter.ai Pricing in 2026

Otter.ai is a cutting-edge AI-powered note-taking and transcription service that helps users capture and organize conversations, meetings, and interviews with ease. With its advanced speech recognition technology, Otter.ai makes it possible to turn spoken words into written text, saving time and increasing productivity.

Pricing Tiers

Otter.ai offers a range of pricing plans to suit different user needs. Here's a breakdown of its current pricing tiers:
  • Free
    • 50 hours of note-taking and transcription per month
    • Basic features, including note-taking, transcription, and search
    • Watermark on exported files
  • Basic
    • $24.99/month (annual), $29.99/month (monthly)
    • Unlimited hours of note-taking and transcription
    • Advanced features, including real-time transcription, live notes, and collaboration
    • No watermarks on exported files
  • Pro
    • $29.99/month (annual), $39.99/month (monthly)
    • Adds advanced features, including priority support and custom branding
  • Enterprise
    • Contact Otter.ai for a custom quote
    • Includes all Pro features, plus advanced security and compliance features

Best Plan for Most Users

For most users, the Basic plan is the sweet spot, offering unlimited hours of note-taking and transcription without breaking the bank. This plan is ideal for individuals and small teams who want to stay organized and productive without the need for advanced features.
