Is Copy.ai Free?

As of 2026, Copy.ai is not entirely free, but it does offer a limited free plan. The free plan allows users to access some of the platform's features, making it a good starting point for those who want to try out the service. However, for full access to all features and capabilities, users need to upgrade to a paid plan.

The free plan includes access to a limited number of templates, as well as the ability to generate a certain number of words per month. Specifically, the free plan allows users to generate up to 1,000 words per month, with limited access to features such as content rewriting and text summarization. While the free plan is a good starting point, it may not be sufficient for heavy users or businesses that require more advanced features.

The paid tier, known as the "Pro" plan, costs $49 per month, billed annually, or $59 per month, billed monthly. This plan includes unlimited word generation, access to all templates, and advanced features such as priority support and the ability to save and reuse content. Additionally, the Pro plan includes features such as multilingual support and the ability to integrate with other tools and platforms.

In verdict, the free tier of Copy.ai is good enough for casual users or those who want to try out the service, but it may not be sufficient for heavy users or businesses. For most users, the free tier will provide a good introduction to the platform's capabilities, but upgrading to the paid plan will be necessary to unlock the full potential of Copy.ai.
