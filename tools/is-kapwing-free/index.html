Is Kapwing Free?

Is Kapwing Free in 2026?

As of 2026, Kapwing offers a mix of free and paid features. The short answer is: yes, Kapwing is free, but with limitations.

What's Free?

The free tier of Kapwing allows users to create and edit videos up to 10 minutes long, with a resolution of up to 1080p. You can also export videos in MP4 format, and Kapwing provides basic editing tools such as trimming, splitting, and merging clips. However, the free tier has some limitations, including: * Limited storage space (100 MB) * No access to advanced features like chroma keying or motion tracking * Kapwing watermark on exported videos

What Costs Money?

Kapwing's paid tier, called "Kapwing Pro," offers additional features and increased storage space. Pricing for Kapwing Pro starts at $15 per month (billed annually), with discounts for teams and enterprise plans. Paid users get access to advanced features like: * Higher video resolution (up to 4K) * More storage space (up to 5 GB) * Removal of the Kapwing watermark * Priority customer support

Verdict

Whether the free tier of Kapwing is good enough for most users depends on their specific needs. If you only need to create short videos for social media or personal projects, the free tier might be sufficient. However, if you need more advanced features or higher video quality, you may want to consider upgrading to Kapwing Pro.
