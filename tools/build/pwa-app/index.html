Deploy Guide

Put Your App on Every Phone — Free

  1. Download your files — Click "Download All Files as ZIP" above and unzip
  2. Upload to free hosting — Use GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel
  3. HTTPS is automatic — All free hosts enable HTTPS (required for PWAs)
  4. Open on your phone — Visit your site URL in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone)
  5. Add to Home Screen — Share button → "Add to Home Screen" on iPhone; 3-dot menu on Android
  6. Done! — Your app icon appears on the home screen just like a native app
