Step-by-Step Guide

How to Install & Publish Your Extension

  1. Generate your files — Fill in the form above and click "Generate My Extension"
  2. Download your files — Click "Download All Files as ZIP"
  3. Unzip the folder — Right-click the downloaded file and click "Extract All"
  4. Open Chrome Extensions — In Chrome, go to chrome://extensions
  5. Enable Developer Mode — Toggle "Developer mode" in the top right corner
  6. Load your extension — Click "Load unpacked" and select your unzipped folder
  7. Test it — Your extension is now running! Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome's toolbar
  8. Publish to Chrome Store — Go to chrome.google.com/webstore/developer/dashboard, pay the one-time $5 fee, and upload your zip
