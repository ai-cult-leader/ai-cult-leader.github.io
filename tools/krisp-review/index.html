Krisp Review 2026

Krisp Review (2026)

Krisp is a noise-cancelling AI tool designed for remote work and video conferencing. It uses machine learning algorithms to eliminate background noise, allowing for seamless communication and focus.

Pros

  • Effective noise cancellation: Krisp delivers on its promise of removing unwanted background noise, making it ideal for noisy environments or shared living spaces.
  • Easy integration: Krisp integrates seamlessly with popular video conferencing platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Skype, making it a convenient addition to your workflow.
  • Customizable noise profiles: Users can create and save custom noise profiles for different environments, allowing for tailored noise cancellation settings.
  • Continuous improvement: Krisp's team actively updates and refines the AI model to improve noise cancellation accuracy and expand its capabilities.

Cons

  • Sensitivity to audio quality: Krisp's performance can be affected by poor audio quality, resulting in subpar noise cancellation.
  • Occasional lag: Some users have reported occasional lag or audio delays when using Krisp, although this is relatively rare.
  • Resource-intensive: Krisp's AI processing can consume a significant amount of system resources, potentially affecting performance on lower-end hardware.

Pricing

Krisp offers a free plan, as well as paid subscriptions starting at $4.99/month (billed annually) for individuals, and $6.99/month (billed annually) for teams.

Who it's best for

Krisp is ideal for remote workers, freelancers, and entrepreneurs who frequently participate in video conferencing calls and need to minimize distractions and improve audio clarity.

Verdict

Krisp is a solid addition to any remote worker's toolset, delivering effective noise cancellation and seamless integration with popular video conferencing platforms. I'd give Krisp a rating of 4.5/5.
