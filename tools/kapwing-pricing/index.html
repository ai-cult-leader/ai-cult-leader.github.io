Kapwing Pricing 2026

Kapwing Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Kapwing is a popular online video editing platform that provides users with a wide range of tools to create, edit, and share engaging video content. With Kapwing, users can trim, split, and merge videos, add text, images, and audio, and even export videos in various formats.

Pricing Tiers

Kapwing offers four pricing tiers to cater to different user needs:
  • Free: This tier is ideal for casual users who want to experiment with Kapwing's features. It includes 1 hour of free video exports per month, limited to 1080p resolution.
  • Basic ($25/month): The Basic tier adds 5 hours of video exports per month, 4K resolution, and priority customer support.
  • Pro ($40/month): The Pro tier includes 20 hours of video exports per month, 4K resolution, priority customer support, and advanced features like video color correction and audio ducking.
  • Enterprise (Custom Pricing): The Enterprise tier is designed for large organizations and enterprises, offering customized pricing, dedicated support, and advanced features like video watermarking and custom branding.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is likely the best plan for most users, offering a balance of features, quality, and affordability. With 20 hours of video exports per month and advanced features, the Pro tier is suitable for individuals and small teams who need to create high-quality video content regularly.

Comparison Note vs Alternatives

Kapwing's pricing is competitive with other online video editing platforms like Lumen5 and InVideo. However, Kapwing's feature set and user interface are more robust, making it a popular choice among creators.
