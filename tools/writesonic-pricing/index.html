Writesonic Pricing 2026

Writesonic Pricing: A Comprehensive Overview

Writesonic is an artificial intelligence (AI) powered content generation tool that helps users create high-quality content quickly and efficiently. With a user-friendly interface and advanced features, Writesonic is a popular choice among marketers, writers, and entrepreneurs.

Writesonic Pricing Tiers

  • Free: Limited to 2,000 words per month, 10 AI models, and basic support.
  • Basic: $15/month (billed annually), 10,000 words per month, 20 AI models, and priority support.
  • Pro: $49/month (billed annually), 50,000 words per month, 50 AI models, and dedicated support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing, tailored to large businesses and enterprises with advanced features and dedicated support.

What Each Tier Includes

The Free tier is suitable for individuals and small businesses, while the Basic tier is ideal for growing businesses. The Pro tier is recommended for established businesses and marketing agencies. The Enterprise tier is designed for large enterprises with complex content generation needs.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is the most popular plan among Writesonic users, offering a balance between affordability and advanced features. This plan is suitable for most users, including marketing agencies and established businesses.
