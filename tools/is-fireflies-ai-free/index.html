Is Fireflies.ai Free?

Is Fireflies.ai Free in 2026?

Fireflies.ai offers a free tier, but with limitations. The service is partially free, allowing users to access some features without paying a fee.

The free tier in Fireflies.ai includes features such as conversation analysis and insights into user engagement. Additionally, users with the free account can create multiple accounts, allowing collaboration and management of multiple chatbots. However, there are limits to the number of messages that can be analyzed in a month, and users are restricted from using custom emojis, GIFs, and other premium features.

For those who require more advanced features or higher limits on messaging analysis, Fireflies.ai offers a paid tier. As of 2026, the paid tier starts at $49 per month for the basic plan, which includes unlimited conversation analysis, custom emojis, GIFs, and more features not available on the free tier. There may be additional pricing tiers and custom pricing for larger teams and enterprises.

The verdict on whether the free tier is good enough for most users depends on their specific needs. For small teams or individuals who only require basic conversation analysis and insights, the free tier may be sufficient. However, for larger teams or those that need more advanced features, the paid tier may be the better option.
