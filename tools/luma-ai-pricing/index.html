Luma AI Pricing 2026

Luma AI Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Breakdown

Luma AI is a cutting-edge AI-powered tool designed to analyze and enhance digital content, images, and videos. With its advanced features and user-friendly interface, Luma AI has become an essential tool for businesses, creators, and individuals looking to elevate their visual content.

Pricing Tiers

Luma AI offers four pricing tiers to cater to different user needs: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise. Here's a breakdown of what each tier includes:
  • Free: Limited to 100 image and video analyses per month. Features include automatic image analysis and basic video editing tools.
  • Basic: Up to 500 analyses per month. Includes additional features such as advanced video editing and 1080p video export.
  • Pro: Up to 5,000 analyses per month. Offers features like 4K video export, team collaboration, and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for high-volume users. Includes all Pro features, plus advanced custom integrations and dedicated support.

Best Plan for Most Users

For most users, the Pro plan is the best choice. It offers a great balance of features and pricing, making it ideal for businesses and individuals who require advanced content analysis and editing tools.
