Is Krisp Free?

Is Krisp Free in 2026?

Krisp offers a free tier in 2026, but with some limitations. The free version is suitable for casual users or those who want to test the platform before upgrading. However, for heavy users or those requiring advanced features, the free tier might not be enough.

What's Free in Krisp?

Krisp's free tier includes features such as:

  • Basic noise cancellation for up to 4 hours
  • Access to a limited number of meeting participants (typically 3-5)
  • Basic call quality and noise reduction
  • Basic dashboard for managing account settings

Please note that the free tier might change over time, and Krisp may introduce new limitations or restrictions without prior notice.

What Costs Money?

Krisp offers a paid tier, called Krisp Premium, which unlocks additional features such as:

  • Premium noise cancellation for up to 12 hours
  • Unlimited meeting participants
  • Advanced call quality and noise reduction
  • Premium dashboard with advanced analytics and features

Krisp Premium pricing starts at $7.99 per month, with discounts available for annual subscriptions.

Verdict: Is the Free Tier Good Enough?

For casual users or those requiring basic noise cancellation, the free tier might be sufficient. However, for heavy users or those requiring advanced features and unlimited meeting participants, the free tier may not be enough. If you're unsure, we recommend trying the free trial to see if Krisp Premium is worth the investment.
