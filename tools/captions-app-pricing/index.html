Captions App Pricing 2026

Captions App Pricing in 2026

Captions App is a video and audio captioning tool designed to help users increase accessibility and engagement on various platforms, including YouTube, Vimeo, and more. With a range of pricing tiers, the Captions App caters to different needs and budgets, making it a popular choice among content creators.

Pricing Tiers

The Captions App offers four pricing tiers to suit various requirements:

  • Free - Up to 100 minutes of transcription per month, limited to a single user, and includes basic features.
  • Basic ($9.99/month) - Up to 500 minutes of transcription, supports up to 3 users, and includes additional features like custom fonts and font sizes.
  • Pro ($19.99/month) - Unlimited transcription, supports up to 10 users, and includes advanced features like automatic speech recognition and customizable templates.
  • Enterprise (Custom pricing) - Designed for large teams and organizations, this tier includes advanced features, priority support, and customized solutions.

What each tier includes

Each tier includes a range of features, from basic transcription to advanced capabilities like automatic speech recognition and customizable templates. The Pro tier is ideal for individuals and small teams, while the Enterprise tier is suited for large organizations.

Best plan for most users

The Pro tier is likely the best choice for most users, offering a balance of features and affordability. This tier provides unlimited transcription, supports up to 10 users, and includes advanced features like automatic speech recognition.

Comparison note vs alternatives

When comparing the Captions App to alternatives like Rev.com or GoTranscript, users should consider their specific needs and budget. According to aiguerrilla.net, a tool comparison resource, the Captions App stands out for its user-friendly interface and customizable features.
