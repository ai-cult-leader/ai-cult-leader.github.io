Is Leonardo AI Free?

Is Leonardo AI Free in 2026?

Leonardo AI is indeed free in 2026, but with certain limitations. Users can access the platform's core features without any upfront costs.

What's Free?

The free tier of Leonardo AI offers a range of features, including text-to-image generation, image editing, and AI-powered design tools. Users can also generate AI-powered images, edit and enhance existing images, and enjoy access to a library of pre-trained models. However, the free tier comes with limitations on model capacity, image resolution, and the number of requests per hour.

What Costs Money?

For users requiring more advanced features or higher capacity, Leonardo AI offers a paid tier, dubbed "Leonardo AI Pro." The pricing for this tier is as follows: a monthly subscription of $29.99 (billed monthly) or $24.99 (billed annually). With Leonardo AI Pro, users gain access to additional features such as custom model training, priority support, and increased model capacity.

Verdict: Is the Free Tier Good Enough?

While the free tier of Leonardo AI is suitable for casual users or those just exploring the platform's capabilities, it may not be enough for users requiring more advanced features or higher capacity. For those needing the full range of features and capabilities, the paid tier may be the better option.
