Is Otter.ai Free?

Is Otter.ai Free in 2026?

Otter.ai is partially free in 2026. While it offers some features without a cost, it also has a paid tier with additional capabilities.

What's Free?

The free tier of Otter.ai includes the following features: - Automated transcription: Otter can transcribe audio and video recordings with high accuracy. - Note-taking: Users can take notes and organize them into folders. - Basic organization: Otter allows users to create teams and invite others to join. - Limited history: The free tier has a 40-minute recording limit and 100 hours of transcription history.

What Costs Money?

Otter.ai's paid tier, known as Otter Business, offers several additional features, including: - Unlimited transcription history - Advanced organization: Users can create custom teams and roles. - Custom branding: Businesses can remove Otter's branding and replace it with their own. - Priority support: Paid users get faster support from Otter's customer support team. - Advanced analytics: Otter Business users can access detailed analytics on their transcription history. Pricing for Otter Business starts at $20 per user per month, billed annually.

Verdict

The free tier of Otter.ai is suitable for casual users or those who only need basic transcription capabilities. However, for businesses or individuals who require more advanced features, the paid tier is likely necessary.
