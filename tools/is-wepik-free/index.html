Is Wepik Free?

Is Wepik Free in 2026?

Wepik offers a free tier, but it's essential to understand what's included and what's not. As of 2026, the answer to whether Wepik is free is yes, but with limitations.

What's Free?

The free tier of Wepik includes basic features such as: - Creating and editing digital comics and graphic novels - Access to a library of templates and design assets - Collaboration features with team members However, there are also limitations to the free tier, including: - Limited storage space (100 MB) - Limited export options (only 2 sizes) - Wepik branding on exported files

What Costs Money?

To access more advanced features and remove limitations, users need to upgrade to a paid tier. As of 2026, Wepik offers two paid plans: - **Pro**: $14.90/month (billed annually) - **Business**: $29.90/month (billed annually) The Pro plan offers 1 GB of storage, additional export options, and the ability to remove Wepik branding. The Business plan adds team management features and priority support.

Verdict

The free tier of Wepik is suitable for casual users and those who only need basic features. However, for professionals and teams, the paid tier offers more value and flexibility.
