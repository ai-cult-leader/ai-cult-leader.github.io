Grammarly Pricing 2026

Pricing for Grammarly in 2026

Grammarly is a popular writing assistant tool that helps users improve their writing skills by detecting grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors. The tool also offers features such as plagiarism detection and suggestions for improvement.

Pricing Tiers

Grammarly offers a range of pricing plans to suit different user needs.

  • Free: This plan includes basic grammar and spelling checks, as well as suggestions for improvement. It's ideal for casual users who want to check their writing for errors.
  • Basic ($11.66/month): This plan includes all the features of the free plan, plus additional features such as plagiarism detection and a more comprehensive grammar check.
  • Pro ($24.95/month): This plan includes all the features of the Basic plan, plus advanced features such as a citation generator and a more in-depth grammar analysis.
  • Enterprise (custom pricing): This plan is designed for businesses and offers advanced features such as team management and integration with popular productivity tools.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is the best option for most users, as it offers a comprehensive set of features that can help improve writing skills and productivity.
