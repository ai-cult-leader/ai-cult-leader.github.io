Is Synthesia Free?

Is Synthesia Free in 2026?

Synthesia offers a free tier, but its capabilities are limited. As of 2026, the free tier allows you to generate up to 5 videos per day, with a maximum length of 5 minutes per video. You can also access limited templates and customization options.

What Costs Money?

To unlock more features and higher video output, you'll need to upgrade to a paid tier. Synthesia offers a range of pricing plans, starting at $15 per user per month for the "Basic" plan. This tier allows for up to 20 videos per day, with a maximum length of 10 minutes per video. The "Pro" plan costs $40 per user per month and offers even more advanced features, including AI-powered editing and higher video resolution.

Verdict: Is the Free Tier Good Enough?

The free tier is suitable for basic use cases, such as creating short, simple videos for social media or internal communication. However, for more complex projects or high-volume video production, the paid tier is likely necessary. If you need to generate a large number of videos with advanced features, upgrading to a paid plan may be worth the investment.
