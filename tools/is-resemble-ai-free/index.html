Is Resemble AI Free?

Is Resemble AI Free in 2026?

Resemble AI offers a free tier, but it does come with some limitations. As of 2026, the free tier is available, allowing users to experiment with the platform's capabilities.

What's Free?

The free tier of Resemble AI includes access to its voice cloning feature, which allows users to generate realistic synthetic voices. It also includes 1 hour of free voice cloning credits per month, which can be used to generate voices for personal projects or testing purposes. Additionally, users can create and manage up to 10 voices, and they can export audio files in various formats, including MP3, WAV, and OGG.

What Costs Money?

To access more advanced features, including unlimited voice cloning credits and additional voices, users must upgrade to a paid tier. Resemble AI offers a tiered pricing model, with the following plans: * Starter: $49/month (billed annually) or $59/month (billed monthly) for 10 voices and 10 hours of voice cloning credits per month * Pro: $149/month (billed annually) or $179/month (billed monthly) for 50 voices and 100 hours of voice cloning credits per month * Business: custom pricing for large-scale applications and enterprises

Verdict

While the free tier of Resemble AI provides a good starting point for experimentation and small projects, it may not be sufficient for more extensive use cases. Users who require more advanced features, higher voice cloning credits, or larger-scale deployments will likely need to upgrade to a paid tier.
