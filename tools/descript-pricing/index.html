Descript Pricing 2026

Descript Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Breakdown

Descript is a powerful audio editing platform that allows users to edit, transcribe, and collaborate on audio content. With its user-friendly interface and advanced features, it has become a go-to tool for podcasters, videocasters, and content creators.

Descript Pricing Tiers

Descript offers four pricing tiers to cater to different user needs: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.

  • Free:
    • 1-hour transcription limit
    • No collaboration features
    • No access to advanced editing features
  • Basic ($12/month):
    • Unlimited transcription
    • Collaboration features
    • Basic editing features
  • Pro ($24/month):
    • Everything in Basic
    • Advanced editing features
    • Priority customer support
  • Enterprise (custom pricing):
    • Everything in Pro
    • Customized onboarding and support
    • Simplified billing and accounting

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is the best option for most users, offering a balance of advanced features, collaboration tools, and priority support at an affordable price.
