Claude Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Claude is an AI-powered knowledge management platform designed to help teams organize, share, and discover information. With its intuitive interface and seamless integration with various tools, Claude has become a popular choice for businesses of all sizes.

Pricing Tiers

Claude offers four pricing tiers to cater to different business needs: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
  • Free: Ideal for small teams or individuals, this tier includes:
    • 100MB storage
    • Unlimited notes and documents
    • Basic search and organization features
    • One-on-one support
  • Basic: Suitable for growing teams, this tier includes:
    • 2GB storage
    • Unlimited notes and documents
    • Advanced search and organization features
    • Premium support
  • Pro: Designed for established businesses, this tier includes:
    • 20GB storage
    • Unlimited notes and documents
    • Customizable workflows and integrations
    • Priority support
  • Enterprise: Tailored for large organizations, this tier includes:
    • Custom storage and features
    • Advanced security and compliance
    • Dedicated support and onboarding
    • Customizable implementation and training

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is likely the best choice for most users, as it offers a balance of features, storage, and support.
