Is Grammarly Free?

As of 2026, Grammarly is not entirely free, but it does offer a free version with limited features. The free version is suitable for basic writing needs, but it lacks the advanced features and support offered by the paid tiers. Whether or not the free version is sufficient depends on the user's specific needs and requirements.

Free Features

The free version of Grammarly includes basic grammar, spelling, and punctuation checks, as well as suggestions for improving sentence structure and clarity. It also offers a limited set of vocabulary enhancements and citation suggestions. However, the free version has limitations, such as not being able to check for plagiarism or offer advanced grammar and syntax checks.

Paid Tiers

The paid tiers of Grammarly, known as Grammarly Premium and Grammarly Business, offer more advanced features, including plagiarism detection, advanced grammar and syntax checks, and enhanced vocabulary suggestions. The pricing for these tiers varies, but as of 2026, Grammarly Premium costs $12 per month for a monthly subscription, while Grammarly Business costs $15 per member per month for an annual subscription.

Verdict

For most casual users, the free version of Grammarly may be sufficient, providing basic writing assistance and suggestions for improvement. However, for students, professionals, or businesses that require more advanced features and support, the paid tiers may be necessary. Ultimately, the choice between the free and paid versions depends on the individual's specific needs and requirements.
