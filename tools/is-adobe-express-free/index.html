Is Adobe Express Free?

Adobe Express Pricing in 2026

Adobe Express is partially free in 2026. Users can access a range of free features and templates, but some advanced features require a paid subscription.

What's Free

With the free tier of Adobe Express, users can access a variety of features, including: - A range of pre-made templates for social media, websites, and other projects - A drag-and-drop interface for designing and editing content - Access to Adobe's library of images, videos, and other assets - Limited export options for web and social media formats

What Costs Money

Adobe Express offers a paid tier, Adobe Express Pro, which unlocks additional features and capabilities. As of 2026, Adobe Express Pro costs: - $9.99 per month, billed monthly - $14.99 per month, billed annually (with a free month's trial) - Free for Adobe Creative Cloud subscribers Adobe Express Pro adds features such as: - Advanced design tools and effects - Unlimited export options for high-resolution files - Collaborative editing and feedback tools

Verdict

Whether the free tier of Adobe Express is good enough for most users depends on their specific needs. If you're looking for a basic tool for creating social media graphics or simple web content, the free tier may be sufficient. However, if you require more advanced design capabilities or high-resolution export options, the paid tier is likely a better choice.
