Is Runway Free?

As of 2026, Runway offers a free tier with limited features. The free version allows users to access basic functionality, but it has restrictions on usage and capabilities. This free tier is suitable for casual users or those who want to try out the platform before committing to a paid plan.

Free Features

The free tier of Runway includes access to a limited set of models, restricted output resolution, and a capped number of requests per day. Specifically, users can expect to have access to a selection of pre-trained models, limited to 256x256 pixel output, and a daily limit of 100 requests. These limitations are in place to ensure that the free tier is used for non-commercial, personal projects, or testing purposes.

Paid Tier

For users who require more advanced features, higher output resolutions, or increased request limits, Runway offers a paid tier. The paid plan, known as "Runway Pro," removes the limitations of the free tier, offering access to a wider range of models, higher output resolutions (up to 1024x1024 pixels), and increased request limits (up to 1000 requests per day). The pricing for Runway Pro is not publicly disclosed, but it is reportedly competitive with other AI platform offerings.

Verdict

The free tier of Runway is suitable for most casual users or those who want to test the platform. However, for commercial use or more demanding projects, the paid tier is likely necessary. The free tier's limitations on output resolution and request limits may be too restrictive for professional users, but it is a good starting point for those who want to explore the platform's capabilities.
