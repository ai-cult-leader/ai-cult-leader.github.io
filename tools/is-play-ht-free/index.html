Is Play.ht Free?

Is Play.ht Free in 2026?

As of 2026, Play.ht does offer a free tier, but with limitations. The short answer is that it's free, but not entirely feature-rich.

On the free tier, you get access to basic text-to-speech (TTS) features, including:

  • 1 hour of free TTS voice credits per day (more than adequate for most personal projects)
  • 1,500 free TTS requests per month
  • Support for a limited set of voice styles and languages

However, for more advanced features and larger projects, you'll need to upgrade to a paid tier. The paid tier offers:

  • Unlimited voice credits (billed monthly or annually)
  • Support for a wider range of voice styles, languages, and customization options
  • Priority customer support

    • The paid tier pricing varies depending on the plan and usage, with a basic plan starting at $25/month (billed annually) and scaling up to enterprise pricing for large-scale applications.

    In terms of whether the free tier is good enough for most users, it largely depends on the scope of your project. If you're working on a small-scale personal project, the free tier should suffice. However, if you're planning to use Play.ht for commercial or large-scale projects, you'll likely need to upgrade to a paid tier for the advanced features and better support.
