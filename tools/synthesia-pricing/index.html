Synthesia Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Breakdown

Synthesia is an AI-powered video generation platform that enables users to create high-quality videos with realistic avatars, lip sync, and voiceovers. The platform offers a range of features and pricing plans to cater to different user needs.

Synthesia Pricing Tiers in 2026

The current pricing tiers for Synthesia in 2026 are as follows:
  • Free
    • Access to a limited library of avatars and templates
    • 1-hour video generation limit
    • No collaboration features
  • Basic ($15/user/month)
    • Access to a larger library of avatars and templates
    • 2-hour video generation limit
    • Basic collaboration features
  • Pro ($30/user/month)
    • Access to the full library of avatars and templates
    • Unlimited video generation
    • Advanced collaboration features
  • Enterprise (custom pricing)
    • Dedicated support and onboarding
    • Custom integrations and workflows
    • Unlimited video generation and collaboration

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is the best option for most users, offering unlimited video generation, advanced collaboration features, and access to the full library of avatars and templates. This plan is ideal for businesses and teams that need to create high-quality videos regularly.
