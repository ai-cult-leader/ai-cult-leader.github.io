Is Captions App Free?

Is Captions App free in 2026?

As of 2026, Captions App offers a mix of free and paid features. To answer the question directly, yes, Captions App is free to use, but with certain limitations.

What's free

The free tier of Captions App includes basic features such as: - Automatic captions for videos and audio files - Transcription and subtitles syncing - Ability to export captions in SRT, VTT, and XML formats However, the free tier also has certain limitations, including: - Limited storage capacity (5 hours of free captions per month) - Watermarked output - No customer support

What costs money

For users who need more features and higher storage capacity, Captions App offers a paid tier called Captions Pro. Pricing for Captions Pro varies depending on the plan chosen: - Monthly plan: $19.99 - Annual plan: $199.99 (billed annually) - Enterprise plan: custom pricing for large teams and organizations The paid tier offers additional features such as: - Unlimited storage capacity - High-quality, professional-grade captions - Priority customer support - Additional export formats

Verdict: is the free tier good enough for most users?

The free tier of Captions App is suitable for users who need basic captioning features and have limited requirements. However, for users who require more storage capacity, professional-grade captions, and priority support, the paid tier may be a better option.
