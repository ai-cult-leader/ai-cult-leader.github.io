Is Descript Free?

Is Descript Free in 2026?

Descript offers a mix of free and paid features, making it a popular choice for content creators, podcasters, and journalists. As of 2026, Descript does provide a free tier, but with limitations.

What's free on Descript includes basic transcription, audio editing, and collaboration tools. You can also export your content in various formats, including MP3, WAV, and more. However, the free tier is limited to 3 hours of transcribed audio per month, and you won't have access to advanced features like custom branding or priority customer support.

Descript's paid tier, Pro, starts at $12 per month, billed annually. This tier removes the audio time limit, adds custom branding, and provides priority customer support. You'll also get access to advanced features like automated transcription, video editing, and more. For teams and larger organizations, Descript offers a Business plan starting at $24 per month, which includes additional features like single sign-on (SSO) and custom roles.

Verdict

The free tier of Descript is suitable for individuals or small projects, but it may not be enough for larger teams or content creators with high audio demands. If you need more advanced features, custom branding, or priority support, the paid tier is the way to go.
