Perplexity Pricing in 2026

Perplexity is an artificial intelligence (AI) tool used for natural language processing and information retrieval. It is designed to provide accurate and informative answers to user queries, making it a valuable resource for various industries such as customer support, research, and e-commerce.

Pricing Tiers

The pricing plan for Perplexity in 2026 includes four main tiers: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.

  • Free: Limited to 100 queries per day, suitable for small-scale projects or personal use.
  • Basic: 1,000 queries per day, additional features such as custom domain and APIs.
  • Pro: 10,000 queries per day, advanced features like integration with third-party tools and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large-scale businesses, including dedicated support and onboarding.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is likely the best option for most users, offering a balance of features and pricing. This tier provides enough queries per day for small to medium-sized businesses, along with advanced features and priority support.
