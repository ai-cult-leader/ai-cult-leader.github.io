Is Ideogram Free?

Is Ideogram Free in 2026?

As of 2026, Ideogram, an AI-powered creative writing assistant, offers a mix of free and paid features. In short, its free tier is available for use, but with certain limitations.

What's free:

The free tier of Ideogram provides access to basic writing assistance, such as grammar and spell checks, as well as some AI-driven writing suggestions. Users can generate text, edit, and even collaborate with others using the free version. However, the free tier includes limitations on the number of characters, text length, and features such as personalized writing recommendations, advanced organization, and some AI integrations.

What costs money:

The paid tier of Ideogram, which includes a 14-day free trial, offers additional features such as unlimited text length, personalized writing recommendations, advanced organization tools, and access to a wider range of AI integrations. Pricing for the paid tier starts at $9.99 per month for a basic plan, increasing to $19.99 per month for the premium plan, and $29.99 per month for the ultimate plan, which includes additional features for teams and large-scale projects.

Verdict:

Whether the free tier of Ideogram is good enough for most users depends on individual needs. If you're looking for basic writing assistance with some AI-driven suggestions, the free tier may suffice. However, if you require advanced features, personalized writing recommendations, or access to a wider range of AI integrations, you may need to upgrade to a paid plan.
