Is Notta Free?

Is Notta Free in 2026?

Notta offers a free tier, but it is subject to certain limitations. As of 2026, the free version of Notta is available, but users should be aware of what features are included and what costs extra.

What's Free?

The free tier of Notta includes features such as basic text editing, note syncing across devices, and limited collaboration capabilities. Users can create and edit notes, as well as organize them using tags and folders. However, the free tier has a limit of 100 notes per user and 1 GB of storage space. Additionally, users on the free tier will see ads in the app.

What Costs Money?

Notta's paid tier, which is called "Notta Premium," offers additional features such as unlimited note storage, increased collaboration capabilities, and the ability to use Notta on mobile devices. The cost of Notta Premium varies depending on the subscription plan chosen, with monthly plans starting at $4.99 and annual plans starting at $49.99.

Verdict: Is the Free Tier Good Enough for Most Users?

The free tier of Notta may be sufficient for users who only need to jot down occasional notes or ideas. However, for those who require more advanced features or higher storage limits, the paid tier may be necessary. Ultimately, the decision to use Notta's free tier or upgrade to the paid tier depends on individual needs and usage patterns.
