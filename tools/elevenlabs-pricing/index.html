ElevenLabs Pricing in 2026

ElevenLabs is a popular AI-powered voice changer and audio editing platform that offers a range of tools to help users customize and enhance their audio content. By leveraging its advanced technology, ElevenLabs enables users to achieve professional-grade audio results without requiring extensive technical expertise. ### Pricing Tiers ElevenLabs offers four pricing tiers to cater to different user needs: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
  • Free: Limited to 1 export per day, 30 minutes of audio editing, and basic voice changer features.
  • Basic: $12.99/month (or $129.99/year), includes 10 exports per day, 4 hours of audio editing, and advanced voice changer features.
  • Pro: $24.99/month (or $249.99/year), includes 50 exports per day, 24 hours of audio editing, and exclusive voice changer effects.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for businesses and organizations requiring advanced features and support.
### What Each Tier Includes - Free: Basic audio editing and voice changer features, limited exports and time. - Basic: Advanced voice changer features, increased exports and editing time. - Pro: Exclusive voice changer effects, expanded exports and editing capacity. - Enterprise: Custom features, support, and pricing for businesses. ### Best Plan for Most Users The Basic plan is likely the best choice for most users, offering a good balance between features and affordability. This tier provides ample exports and editing time, making it suitable for frequent users. ### Comparison Note When comparing ElevenLabs to its alternatives, such as AIVA or Auphonic, it's worth noting that ElevenLabs stands out for its ease of use and extensive voice changer capabilities.
