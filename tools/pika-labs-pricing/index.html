Pika Labs Pricing 2026

Pika Labs Pricing 2026: A Breakdown

Pika Labs is a cloud-based customer relationship management (CRM) and sales automation platform designed to help businesses streamline their sales processes and enhance customer interactions. With a user-friendly interface and AI-powered tools, Pika Labs aims to simplify sales management for teams of all sizes.

Pricing Tiers

Pika Labs offers four pricing tiers to cater to various business needs. Here's a summary of each tier:
  • Free: Ideal for small businesses or individuals, the Free plan includes 10 users, 10,000 records, and basic features like lead and contact management.
  • Basic: Starting at $19/user/month (billed annually), the Basic plan includes 20 users, 50,000 records, and additional features like pipeline management and sales forecasting.
  • Pro: Priced at $49/user/month (billed annually), the Pro plan includes unlimited users, 500,000 records, and advanced features like AI-powered lead scoring and sales automation.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large businesses, the Enterprise plan includes all Pro features, plus dedicated support and onboarding.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is likely the best option for most businesses, offering a balance of features and affordability. However, the Enterprise plan may be a better fit for large organizations with complex sales operations.
