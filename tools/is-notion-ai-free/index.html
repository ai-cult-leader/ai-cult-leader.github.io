Is Notion AI Free?

Notion AI is not entirely free in 2026, as it offers a mix of free and paid features. The free version provides limited access to AI-powered tools, while the paid tier unlocks more advanced capabilities. Users can still utilize some Notion AI features without paying, but with certain restrictions.

The free version of Notion AI includes features such as basic text analysis, suggestions, and content generation, with limitations on the number of requests per day. Additionally, free users can access a limited set of templates and models, which can be useful for simple tasks and personal projects. However, the free tier has restrictions on the amount of data that can be processed and the frequency of API requests.

The paid tier of Notion AI, on the other hand, offers more advanced features such as custom model training, priority support, and increased API request limits. The pricing for the paid tier is not publicly disclosed, but it is expected to be competitive with other AI-powered productivity tools. Paid users can also access more templates, models, and priority customer support, making it a more comprehensive solution for businesses and power users.

In verdict, the free tier of Notion AI is good enough for most casual users who need basic AI-powered tools for personal projects or simple tasks. However, power users and businesses may find the limitations of the free tier restrictive and require the paid tier for more advanced features and capabilities.
