Suno Pricing 2026

Suno Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Review

Suno is a cloud-based collaboration platform designed to streamline team communication and workflow management. With a user-friendly interface and robust features, Suno has gained popularity among businesses of all sizes.

Pricing Tiers

Suno offers four pricing plans to cater to different business needs. Here's a breakdown of each tier:
  • Free:
    • 1GB storage
    • 10 users
    • Basic features
  • Basic ($5/user/month, $40/month for 10 users):
    • 10GB storage
    • 20 users
    • Advanced features (file sharing, task management)
  • Pro ($15/user/month, $120/month for 10 users):
    • 50GB storage
    • 50 users
    • Additional features (video conferencing, reporting analytics)
  • Enterprise (custom pricing for large businesses):
    • Unlimited storage
    • Unlimited users
    • Premium features (single sign-on, data compliance)

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is ideal for most users, offering a balance of features and affordability. With 50GB storage, 50 users, and advanced features like video conferencing, it's perfect for medium-sized businesses or teams.
