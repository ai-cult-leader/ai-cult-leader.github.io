Notion AI Pricing 2026

Notion AI Pricing in 2026

Notion AI is a highly versatile tool that combines the features of a note-taking app, project management platform, and AI-powered assistant. It allows users to create custom databases, collaborate with others, and leverage AI-driven insights to streamline their workflow.

Pricing Tiers

Notion AI offers a range of pricing plans to suit different user needs:
  • Free:
    • 1 person limit
    • 100MB storage
    • Basic templates and features
  • Basic:
    • Up to 5 people
    • 5GB storage
    • Additional features like blocks, templates, and more
  • Pro:
    • Up to 100 people
    • 100GB storage
    • Advanced features like team collaboration, templates, and AI-powered tools
  • Enterprise:
    • Custom pricing for large teams and organizations
    • 1TB storage and more
    • All features, plus advanced security and support

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is likely the best choice for most users, offering a great balance of features and affordability. With up to 100 people and 100GB storage, it's ideal for small to medium-sized teams.
