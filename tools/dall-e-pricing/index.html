Pricing Tiers

The current pricing tiers for DALL-E in 2026 are as follows:

  • Free: Limited to 25 image generations per month, suitable for personal or hobbyist use.
  • Basic: $15/month, 100 image generations, access to premium features like batch processing and advanced editing tools.

What Each Tier Includes

The Basic tier is the most suitable for most users, offering a balance of affordability and feature richness. The Pro tier is geared towards professionals and power users, while the Enterprise tier is designed for large teams and businesses with high image generation demands.
