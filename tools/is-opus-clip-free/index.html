Is Opus Clip Free?

Is Opus Clip Free in 2026?

Opus Clip is free to use, with some limitations. In 2026, users can access the basic features of the AI-powered content creation tool without paying a dime.

What's free with Opus Clip? Users can create clips, edit them with the built-in editor, and export them in various formats. The free tier also includes basic AI-powered features like auto-captions and automatic music suggestions. However, there are limits on export quality, the number of exports per month, and access to advanced AI features.

What costs money with Opus Clip? The paid tier, Opus Clip Pro, unlocks more advanced AI features, higher export quality, and increased export limits. As of my knowledge cutoff in 2023, the pricing for Opus Clip Pro is $14.99/month (billed annually) or $19.99/month (billed monthly). However, please note that pricing may have changed since my knowledge cutoff.

Verdict: is the free tier good enough for most users? The free tier is suitable for users who want to experiment with Opus Clip or create basic content for personal use. However, if you need high-quality exports, advanced AI features, or increased export limits, you may need to upgrade to the paid tier.
