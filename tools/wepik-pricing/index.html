Wepik Pricing 2026

Wepik Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Overview

Wepik is a visual storytelling platform that enables users to create digital comics, cartoons, and other multimedia content. With a user-friendly interface and advanced features, Wepik has become a popular choice for creators and businesses alike.

Pricing Tiers

Wepik offers four pricing tiers: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise. Below is a summary of each tier's features and pricing:
  • Free: Free version includes 3 projects, 10 pages per project, and limited collaboration features. Pricing: Free.
  • Basic: Basic plan includes 10 projects, 20 pages per project, and additional collaboration features. Pricing: $12/month (billed annually).
  • Pro: Pro plan includes unlimited projects and pages, advanced collaboration features, and additional export formats. Pricing: $30/month (billed annually).
  • Enterprise: Enterprise plan includes all Pro features, priority support, and custom onboarding. Pricing: Custom quote.

BEST PLAN FOR MOST USERS

The Pro plan is the most popular choice among Wepik users, offering a balance of features and affordability. For most users, the Pro plan provides the necessary tools to create and collaborate on visual content without breaking the bank.
