Resemble AI Pricing 2026

Resemble AI Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Breakdown

Resemble AI is an AI-powered voice cloning platform that enables users to create realistic, synthetic voices. This innovative technology has various applications, including voice assistants, gaming, and content creation.

Pricing Tiers

Resemble AI offers four pricing tiers to suit different user needs: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
  • Free: 1,000 characters per day, limited to personal use, and subject to certain usage restrictions.
  • Basic: $25/month (billed annually), 10,000 characters per day, and includes voice cloning, audio editing, and 24/7 support.
  • Pro: $100/month (billed annually), 100,000 characters per day, and adds advanced features like custom branding, priority support, and dedicated account management.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large-scale deployments, with features tailored to meet specific business needs.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Basic plan should suffice for most users, offering a good balance of features and affordability. If you plan to use Resemble AI for personal projects or small-scale commercial applications, the Basic plan is the most cost-effective option.
