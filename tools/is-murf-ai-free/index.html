Is Murf AI Free?

Is Murf AI Free in 2026?

Murf AI offers a free tier, but with significant limitations on usage. In 2026, the service remains largely accessible without a fee.

What's Free?

The free version of Murf AI includes access to a range of features such as basic editing, voiceover and text-to-speech functionality, as well as the ability to upload media files. However, there are limits to the number of projects and minutes of output that can be generated each month. Additionally, the free tier may include Murf's watermark on output files.

What Costs Money?

To access more advanced features, remove Murf's watermark, or increase project and output limits, users must subscribe to a paid tier. The paid tier offers more minutes of output, priority customer support, and additional features such as advanced editing and more control over voiceover options. As of 2026, pricing for Murf AI's paid tier is $19 per user per month for the basic plan, increasing to $39 per user per month for the premium plan.

Verdict

The free tier is likely sufficient for casual users or those with simple requirements, but heavy users or those needing more advanced features will likely require the paid tier. Users should evaluate their needs and compare services to determine the best fit for their budget and requirements.
