Pictory Pricing for 2026: A Comprehensive Overview

Pictory is a powerful video creation platform that allows users to turn blog posts, articles, and social media content into engaging videos. Pricing Tiers:

Pictory offers four pricing plans: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise. Here's a breakdown of what each tier includes:

  • Free: Unlimited videos, 10 minutes video length, basic features, and Pictory branding.
  • Basic: Unlimited videos, 60 minutes video length, basic features, and Pictory branding. ($15/month)
  • Pro: Unlimited videos, 120 minutes video length, advanced features, and custom branding. ($49/month)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large teams and enterprises, with advanced features, custom branding, and dedicated support.

Best Plan for Most Users:

The Pro plan is the best option for most users, offering a balance between features and affordability. It's ideal for individuals and small businesses looking to create high-quality videos without breaking the bank.
