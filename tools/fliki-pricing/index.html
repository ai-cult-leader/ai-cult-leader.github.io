Fliki Pricing 2026

Fliki Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Review

Fliki is a user-friendly project management and collaboration tool designed to help teams streamline their workflow and achieve their goals. With its intuitive interface and robust features, Fliki has become a popular choice among businesses and individuals alike.

Fliki Pricing Tiers

Fliki offers four pricing tiers to cater to different user needs: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
  • Free: Limited to 3 projects, 10 users, and basic features.
  • Basic: $4/user/month (billed annually), 10 projects, 20 users, and essential features like task management and file sharing.
  • Pro: $8/user/month (billed annually), unlimited projects, 50 users, and advanced features like Gantt charts, custom workflows, and integrations.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large teams and organizations, with additional features like single sign-on, advanced security, and dedicated support.

What Each Tier Includes

The Free tier is ideal for small teams or individuals who need a simple project management tool. The Basic tier is suitable for growing teams who require more features and users. The Pro tier is best for teams who need advanced features and customization options. The Enterprise tier is designed for large organizations with complex needs.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is likely the best plan for most users, offering a balance of features, users, and price. However, the Basic tier may be sufficient for smaller teams or those on a tighter budget.

Comparison Note vs Alternatives

When compared to other project management tools like Trello and Asana, Fliki offers a more comprehensive feature set and a user-friendly interface.
