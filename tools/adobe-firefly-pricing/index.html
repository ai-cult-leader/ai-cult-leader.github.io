Adobe Firefly Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Adobe Firefly is an AI-powered image and video editing platform that simplifies the creative process by offering a suite of tools and features to enhance and manipulate visual content. With Firefly, users can access a vast library of images, videos, and effects, and edit them with ease using a user-friendly interface.

Pricing Tiers

Adobe Firefly offers four pricing tiers: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise. Here's a breakdown of what each tier includes:
  • Free: 2 GB of storage, access to a limited library of stock images and videos, and basic editing features.
  • Basic: 5 GB of storage, access to a larger library of stock images and videos, and additional editing features, including object removal and text editing.
  • Pro: 10 GB of storage, access to a comprehensive library of stock images and videos, and advanced editing features, including AI-powered color grading and motion graphics.
  • Enterprise: Custom storage and library access, advanced editing features, and dedicated customer support.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is likely the best plan for most users, offering a balance of storage, library access, and advanced editing features that cater to both personal and professional needs.
