Vidyo.ai Pricing 2026

Vidyo.ai Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Review

Vidyo.ai is an AI-powered video conferencing platform designed for businesses, offering features like automated transcription, sentiment analysis, and facial recognition. Its advanced technology provides users with a seamless and engaging experience, making it an attractive option for companies of all sizes.

Pricing Tiers

Pricing Tiers:
  • Free: Ideal for small teams and personal use, this tier is limited to 1:1 video conferencing and basic features.
  • Basic: Suitable for small businesses, this tier offers 25 concurrent meetings, automated transcription, and basic analytics.
  • Pro: Geared towards medium-sized businesses, this tier includes 100 concurrent meetings, advanced analytics, and sentiment analysis.
  • Enterprise: Designed for large enterprises, this tier offers custom scalability, advanced features, and dedicated support.

What Each Tier Includes

- Free: 1:1 video conferencing, automated transcription - Basic: 25 concurrent meetings, automated transcription, basic analytics - Pro: 100 concurrent meetings, advanced analytics, sentiment analysis - Enterprise: Custom scalability, advanced features, dedicated support

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is the best option for most users, offering a balance of features and scalability for medium-sized businesses.
