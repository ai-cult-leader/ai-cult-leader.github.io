HeyGen Pricing 2026

HeyGen Pricing in 2026

HeyGen is a cloud-based video creation platform that enables users to create professional-grade videos without requiring extensive video production expertise. With its intuitive interface and array of templates, HeyGen has become a popular choice for individuals and businesses looking to elevate their video content.

Pricing Tiers

HeyGen offers four pricing tiers to cater to different user needs:
  • Free:
    • 500MB storage
    • 2 hours of rendering time
    • Basic templates and features
  • Basic ($14/month):
    • 2GB storage
    • 6 hours of rendering time
    • More advanced templates and features
  • Pro ($29/month):
    • 10GB storage
    • 12 hours of rendering time
    • Custom templates and features
    • Priority support
  • Enterprise (custom pricing):
    • Unlimited storage
    • Custom rendering time
    • Custom templates and features
    • Dedicated support

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro tier is likely the best plan for most users, offering a good balance of features and affordability.

Comparison Note vs Alternatives

HeyGen's pricing is competitive with other video creation platforms on the market. However, users may want to explore alternative options, such as Lumen5 or InVideo, to determine which platform best meets their specific needs.
