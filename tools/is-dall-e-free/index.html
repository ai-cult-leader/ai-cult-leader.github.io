Is DALL-E Free?

As of 2026, DALL-E is not entirely free, but it does offer a limited free tier. The free version allows users to generate a certain number of images per month without incurring any costs. However, the free tier comes with significant limitations, making it less suitable for heavy users or those requiring high-volume image generation.

The free tier of DALL-E includes access to the model's basic features, such as text-to-image generation and limited editing capabilities. Users can generate a restricted number of images per month, currently set at 50 images, after which they need to upgrade to a paid plan. The free tier is ideal for casual users who want to experiment with the technology or create occasional social media posts.

For users who require more features, higher image generation limits, or priority support, DALL-E offers a paid tier. The paid plan, known as DALL-E+, costs $15 per month for 1150 images, with additional images available at $0.02 per image. This tier also includes extra features like larger image sizes, more advanced editing tools, and priority customer support.

In verdict, the free tier of DALL-E is sufficient for most casual users who only need to generate a few images per month. However, heavy users, businesses, or professionals may find the limitations of the free tier restrictive and require the paid tier for their needs.
