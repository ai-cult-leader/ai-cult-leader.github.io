Is NightCafe Free?

Is NightCafe Free in 2026?

Yes, NightCafe remains partially free in 2026, but with limited features and usage. The platform offers a basic level of access to its AI art generation capabilities without requiring payment.

What's Free?

The free tier of NightCafe allows users to create AI-generated art using the platform's AI model. However, it comes with some limitations, including:

- Limited image resolution (typically 512x512 pixels)

- Limited AI usage (may not be suitable for complex or high-demand projects)

- Occasional watermarks on generated images

While these limitations may be a hindrance for some users, they can still create decent AI art for personal or low-stakes projects within the free tier.

What Costs Money?

NightCafe's paid tier offers advanced features, increased AI usage, and high-resolution image generation. The pricing for NightCafe's paid tier is as follows:

- A monthly subscription costs $9.99 (or $99.99 per year)

The paid tier is suitable for users who require high-quality images, extensive AI usage, or no watermarks on their generated art.

Verdict

The free tier of NightCafe is suitable for casual users or those with basic requirements. However, users with more demanding needs, such as professional artists or high-volume projects, may find the free tier's limitations a significant drawback.
