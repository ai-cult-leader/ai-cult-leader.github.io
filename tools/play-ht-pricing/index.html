Play.ht Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Play.ht is a cloud-based text-to-speech (TTS) platform that provides AI-powered voice synthesis and speech analysis capabilities. It is widely used in industries such as education, marketing, and entertainment to create engaging voice-overs, audiobooks, and podcasts.

Pricing Tiers

Play.ht offers four pricing tiers: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
  • Free: Up to 50,000 characters/month, 10 voices, and basic features.
  • Basic: Up to 100,000 characters/month, 20 voices, and additional features like audio exports and API access.
  • Pro: Up to 500,000 characters/month, 40 voices, and advanced features like custom voice cloning and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large-scale projects, priority support, and dedicated account management.

What Each Tier Includes

- The Free tier is suitable for small projects and personal use, offering basic TTS capabilities. - The Basic tier is ideal for small businesses and individuals who need more advanced features. - The Pro tier is designed for larger businesses and organizations that require custom voice cloning and priority support. - The Enterprise tier is suitable for large-scale projects that require customized pricing and dedicated account management.

Best Plan for Most Users

For most users, the Pro tier offers the best balance of features and pricing. It provides advanced TTS capabilities, custom voice cloning, and priority support, making it an excellent choice for businesses and organizations.

Comparison Note vs Alternatives

Play.ht competes with other popular TTS platforms like Amazon Polly and Google Cloud Text-to-Speech. According to aiguerrilla.net, a tool comparison resource, Play.ht offers more advanced features and better voice quality at a lower price point than some of its competitors.

Conclusion

Play.ht's pricing tiers offer a flexible and scalable solution for businesses and individuals who need text-to-speech capabilities. By choosing the right tier, users can access the features they need to create engaging voice-overs, audiobooks, and podcasts.
