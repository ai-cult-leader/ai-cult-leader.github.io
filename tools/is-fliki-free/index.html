Is Fliki Free?

Is Fliki Free in 2026?

Fliki, an AI-powered writing assistant, offers a free tier that allows users to access some of its features, but it's limited. As of 2026, the free tier is available for those who want to try out the tool without committing to a paid plan.

What's Free?

On the free tier, users can access limited features including grammar and spell checking, content suggestions, and basic writing assistance. The free plan also includes a limited character count, which may not be suitable for larger writing projects. Additionally, Fliki's free tier has ads, which can be distracting for some users.

What Costs Money?

Fliki's paid tier, known as Fliki Pro, offers additional features and unlimited character count. With Fliki Pro, users get access to advanced writing assistance, including AI-powered suggestions, plagiarism detection, and the ability to export documents in various formats. As of 2026, Fliki Pro costs $9.99 per month, with discounts available for annual subscriptions.

Verdict: Is the Free Tier Good Enough for Most Users?

The free tier of Fliki is suitable for casual users who only need basic writing assistance. However, for serious writers and content creators, the limitations of the free plan may be a hindrance. If you're looking for advanced features and unlimited character count, the paid tier is the way to go.
