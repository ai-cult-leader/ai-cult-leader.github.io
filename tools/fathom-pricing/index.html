Fathom Pricing 2026

Fathom Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Breakdown

Fathom is a popular website analytics tool designed for those looking for a more lightweight and simple alternative to other analytics platforms. It offers real-time tracking, customizable dashboards, and detailed analytics reports to help users understand their website traffic.

Pricing Tiers in 2026

Fathom offers four pricing plans: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.

  • Free: Limited to 1000 page views per month, ideal for small websites or personal projects.
  • Basic: Starting at $14/month (billed annually), includes 10,000 page views per month, custom tracking, and support.
  • Pro: Starting at $59/month (billed annually), includes 50,000 page views per month, advanced tracking, and priority support.
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large businesses or organizations with advanced requirements.

What Each Tier Includes

Each tier includes a set of standard features, with additional features and increased page view limits as you upgrade.

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is the most popular option for most users, offering a good balance between features and cost.

Comparison Note vs Alternatives

Fathom's pricing is competitive with other analytics tools like Google Analytics and Matomo, but its focus on simplicity and ease-of-use sets it apart.
