Final Cut Pro Pricing 2026

Final Cut Pro Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Final Cut Pro is a professional video editing software designed for Mac users, offering a wide range of advanced features and tools for creating stunning visuals. Developed by Apple, it has become a popular choice among videographers, filmmakers, and content creators.

Pricing Tiers

In 2026, Final Cut Pro offers four pricing tiers:
  • Free: A limited version of the software, suitable for hobbyists and beginners. This tier includes basic editing features, but lacks advanced tools and effects.
  • Basic: Starting at $19.99/month, this tier includes additional features like color grading and audio editing. It's ideal for small businesses and independent creators.
  • Pro: Priced at $49.99/month, this tier offers the full range of features, including 3D and 360-degree video editing. It's the go-to choice for professional videographers and filmmakers.
  • Enterprise: A custom pricing plan for large organizations and businesses, offering advanced features and support. This tier is best suited for corporate clients and production companies.

What's Included in Each Tier

- Free: Basic editing features, limited effects, and no advanced tools. - Basic: Color grading, audio editing, and additional effects. - Pro: Full range of features, including 3D and 360-degree video editing. - Enterprise: Custom pricing plan with advanced features and support.

Best Plan for Most Users

For most users, the Pro tier ($49.99/month) is the best choice, offering the full range of features and tools for professional video editing.
