Opus Clip Pricing 2026

Opus Clip Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Review

Opus Clip is a versatile note-taking tool designed for users who require flexibility and seamless organization in their digital note-taking experience. With features like customizable tags, drag-and-drop functionality, and robust note export options, Opus Clip has become a popular choice among professionals and students alike.

Pricing Tiers

The pricing structure of Opus Clip offers various plans catering to different user needs.

  • Free Plan: Basic features, limited to 2 devices, 1 GB storage, and minimal customization options.
  • Basic Plan ($4.99/month or $49.99/year): Includes features like unlimited devices, 2 GB storage, and customizable tags.
  • Pro Plan ($9.99/month or $99.99/year): Adds advanced features like drag-and-drop functionality, note export options, and 5 GB storage.
  • Enterprise Plan (custom pricing): Designed for large-scale teams and organizations, offering advanced security features, dedicated support, and higher storage limits.
For most users, the Basic Plan provides an excellent balance between features and affordability. It includes essential features like customizable tags and unlimited devices, while also offering more storage than the free plan.
