Is Pictory Free?

Is Pictory Free in 2026?

Pictory offers a free tier, but it comes with limitations. While users can still create and edit videos, there are restrictions on advanced features and exports.

What's Free?

The free tier allows users to create videos up to 60 seconds in length with a resolution of 1080p. It includes 500 free video edits and supports up to 10 video tracks. Users can also access 500 free templates and a library of stock media. Additionally, the free tier includes basic audio edits and color adjustments.

What Costs Money?

Pictory's paid tier, Pictory Pro, offers advanced features such as unlimited exports, 4K resolution, and more advanced audio and color editing tools. The Pro tier also includes additional templates, stock media, and priority customer support. Pricing for Pictory Pro starts at $29 per month, with discounts available for annual payments.

Verdict

The free tier of Pictory is suitable for casual users and those who only need basic video editing features. However, for more advanced users or those who require high-quality exports, the paid tier may be necessary. Users should weigh their needs and compare pricing with other AI-powered video editing tools to determine the best option.
