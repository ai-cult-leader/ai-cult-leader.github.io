Is Writesonic Free?

As of 2026, Writesonic offers a limited free version of its AI-powered writing tool. The free tier allows users to access basic features, but with certain limitations. While it's not completely free, the limited version can still be useful for casual users or those who want to try out the tool before committing to a paid plan.

Free Features and Limits

The free version of Writesonic includes access to its AI writer, content rewriter, and text expander, but with limited output and usage. Users can generate a certain number of words per month, and the output is limited to a few hundred words at a time. Additionally, the free tier may include ads and have limited support options.

Paid Tier and Pricing

The paid tier of Writesonic, which starts at around $10-15 per month, offers unlimited word generation, priority support, and access to more advanced features like SEO optimization and content analysis. The exact pricing may vary depending on the plan and the number of users, but the paid tier is geared towards heavy users, businesses, and professionals who need more advanced features and higher output limits.

Verdict

For most casual users, the free tier of Writesonic may be sufficient, especially if they only need to generate short pieces of content occasionally. However, for heavy users or those who require more advanced features, the paid tier is likely a better option. Ultimately, the free tier is a good starting point, but users may find themselves upgrading to the paid plan as their needs grow.
