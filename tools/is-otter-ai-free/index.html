Is Otter.ai Free?

Is Otter.ai Free in 2026?

Otter.ai is partially free in 2026, offering a range of features but with certain limitations. The free tier is suitable for basic needs but may not be sufficient for heavy users.

What's Free?

The free tier of Otter.ai includes: - Up to 10 hours of audio recording per month - Basic transcription and note-taking features - Integration with popular cloud storage services - Limited access to Otter's premium features

What Costs Money?

Otter.ai's paid tier, Otter for Teams (formerly known as Otter Business), includes: - Unlimited audio recording hours - Advanced transcription and editing features - Priority customer support - Customization options for teams and organizations - Pricing for Otter for Teams varies depending on the plan chosen, with a starting cost of $20 per user per month (billed annually).

Verdict

While the free tier of Otter.ai is suitable for occasional or light users, heavy users may find the 10-hour limit restrictive. For those requiring advanced features and unlimited recording hours, the paid tier of Otter for Teams is the better option.
