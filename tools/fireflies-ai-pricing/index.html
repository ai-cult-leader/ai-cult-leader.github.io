Fireflies.ai Pricing in 2026

Fireflies.ai is an innovative AI-based video meeting summarization platform designed to streamline workflow, improve productivity, and reduce meeting fatigue. By using AI to summarize video meetings, users can quickly review key points, decisions, and actions without having to watch the full recording.

Pricing Tiers

Fireflies.ai offers four pricing tiers: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.
  • Free: Up to 30 minutes of video meeting summary per month, limited to 1 user
  • Basic ($6/user/month): Up to 60 minutes of video meeting summary per month, 2 users, basic support
  • Pro ($12/user/month): Up to 120 minutes of video meeting summary per month, 10 users, priority support, and advanced features
  • Enterprise (custom pricing): Unlimited video meeting summary, custom user accounts, dedicated support, and integration with other tools

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is ideal for most users, offering a balance of features, support, and pricing. This plan provides enough summary minutes for a small team, advanced features, and priority support.
