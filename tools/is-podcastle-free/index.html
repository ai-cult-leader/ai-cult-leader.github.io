Is Podcastle Free?

Is Podcastle Free in 2026?

As of 2026, Podcastle offers both a free tier and a paid subscription. The good news is that the free tier is indeed available, but it comes with some limitations. While it provides access to the core features, users will be restricted to a certain level of performance.

What's Free?

The free tier of Podcastle includes basic features such as AI-powered writing tools, a text-to-speech system, and some customization options. However, it also has limitations on the number of documents that can be processed, the number of voices that can be used, and the overall speed of the platform.

What Costs Money?

Podcastle's paid tier, which starts at $19.99/month, unlocks additional features and removes the limitations imposed on the free tier. This paid subscription also includes priority customer support, advanced customization options, and a higher document processing limit.

Verdict

For casual users who only need basic AI writing tools, the free tier of Podcastle may be sufficient. However, for those who require more advanced features or higher performance, the paid subscription is the better choice.
