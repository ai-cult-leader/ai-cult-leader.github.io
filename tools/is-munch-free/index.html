Is Munch Free?

Is Munch free in 2026?

Munch is free to use in 2026, but with some limitations.

What's free in Munch?

The free tier of Munch offers a basic set of features, including: - Limited AI-powered writing suggestions - Access to a limited library of templates - Ability to create and edit documents - Limited collaboration features

What costs money in Munch?

Munch offers a paid tier, Munch Pro, which unlocks additional features and removes the limitations of the free tier. The pricing for Munch Pro is as follows: - Monthly: $9.99 - Annually: $99.99 - Business Plan: $14.99 per user/month (billed annually) The paid tier includes features such as: - Advanced AI-powered writing suggestions - Access to a comprehensive library of templates - Unlimited collaboration features - Priority support

Verdict: is the free tier good enough for most users?

While the free tier of Munch offers a basic set of features, it may not be sufficient for power users or those who require advanced features. For casual users, the free tier may be sufficient, but those who need more advanced features may need to upgrade to the paid tier.
