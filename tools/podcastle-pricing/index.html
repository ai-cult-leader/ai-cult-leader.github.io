Podcastle Pricing 2026

Podcastle Pricing in 2026: A Breakdown of Plans and Features

Podcastle is a popular podcast hosting and management platform that caters to podcasters of all levels, from individual creators to large teams and media companies. With its user-friendly interface and robust set of features, Podcastle has become a go-to choice for many podcasters worldwide.

Pricing Tiers

Podcastle offers four pricing tiers to suit various needs and budgets: Free, Basic, Pro, and Enterprise.

  • Free: 1 podcast, 1 user, 500MB storage, and basic features
  • Basic: 1 podcast, 5 users, 2GB storage, and additional features like custom domains and analytics
  • Pro: 10 podcasts, 15 users, 10GB storage, and features like advanced analytics, team collaboration, and priority support
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing for large teams and media companies with advanced features and dedicated support

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is the most popular and recommended plan for most podcasters, offering a good balance of features, storage, and users. It's ideal for independent podcasters, small teams, and businesses with multiple podcasts.

Comparison Note

Podcastle's pricing is competitive with other popular podcast hosting platforms like Buzzsprout and Podbean. However, its feature set and user interface set it apart from others in the market.
