Is Fathom Free?

Is Fathom Free in 2026?

Fathom, a popular analytics tool for websites, offers a free tier in 2026. However, it comes with certain limitations and features that are only available to paid subscribers. The free tier is suitable for personal projects or small websites with limited traffic.

What's Free

The free tier of Fathom includes: * Basic analytics data such as page views, unique visitors, and bounce rate * A simple and easy-to-use interface for tracking website metrics * Limited data retention (30 days of data) * No real-time analytics

What Costs Money

Fathom's paid tier starts at $5 per month and offers additional features such as: * Unlimited data retention * Real-time analytics * Customizable dashboards * Advanced tracking features for e-commerce sites * Priority support

Verdict

The free tier of Fathom is suitable for personal projects or small websites with limited traffic. However, for larger websites or businesses that require more advanced analytics and tracking features, the paid tier is necessary.
