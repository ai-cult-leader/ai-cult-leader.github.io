Is InVideo AI Free?

Is InVideo AI free in 2026?

InVideo AI does offer a free plan in 2026, allowing users to utilize its video editing capabilities to some extent.

The free tier in InVideo AI comes with basic features such as access to a vast template library, drag-and-drop functionality, and the ability to export videos in various resolutions. Additionally, users can utilize the AI-powered editing tools, including automatic text-to-speech, background music suggestions, and color grading. However, there are limitations in place, such as a 300MB video export limit, the inability to use advanced features like chroma keying, and a watermark on exported videos.

For users requiring more advanced features, such as higher video export limits, chroma keying, and the ability to remove the watermark, a paid subscription is required. As of 2026, InVideo AI's paid tier costs $13.50 per month, with discounts available for annual subscriptions and free trials.

Based on the features and limitations of the free tier, it appears that InVideo AI's free plan is suitable for casual users, such as students or small business owners who only occasionally need to edit videos. However, for professionals or those requiring more advanced features, a paid subscription may be necessary.
