Is Suno Free?

Suno is not entirely free in 2026, as it offers a mix of free and paid features. The free version has limitations, but it still provides a decent set of tools for basic use. While some features are accessible without a subscription, others require a paid plan.

The free version of Suno includes access to limited AI models, restricted data storage, and basic editing capabilities. These features are suitable for casual users or those who want to test the platform before committing to a paid subscription. However, the free tier has limitations on the number of projects, collaboration features, and Priority support.

The paid tier of Suno, on the other hand, offers unlimited access to AI models, increased data storage, advanced editing capabilities, and priority support. As of 2026, the pricing for the paid tier is not publicly disclosed, but it is expected to be competitive with other AI tool providers. The paid plan is geared towards power users, businesses, and professionals who require more advanced features and support.

For most users, the free tier of Suno may be sufficient, especially if they only need basic features and are not concerned about data storage or collaboration. However, power users or businesses may find the paid tier more suitable for their needs. Ultimately, the choice between the free and paid tiers depends on the individual's or organization's specific requirements.
