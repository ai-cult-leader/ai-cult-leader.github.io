Is Microsoft Copilot Free?

As of 2026, Microsoft Copilot is not entirely free, but it does offer a limited free tier. The free version provides basic features, allowing users to get a taste of the AI-powered productivity tool. However, for full functionality, users need to subscribe to the paid tier.

The free tier of Microsoft Copilot includes access to a limited set of features, such as basic text suggestions and simple data analysis. Users can also utilize the tool for a restricted number of tasks per month. While these features can be helpful for casual users, they may not be sufficient for power users or businesses that rely heavily on the tool.

The paid tier of Microsoft Copilot, on the other hand, offers advanced features like enhanced text suggestions, complex data analysis, and integration with other Microsoft tools. The pricing for the paid tier varies depending on the user's needs, with individual plans starting at a few dollars per month and business plans costing significantly more. As of 2026, the exact pricing is as follows: $9.99 per month for the basic plan and $19.99 per month for the premium plan.

Verdict: the free tier of Microsoft Copilot can be sufficient for casual users who only need basic features. However, for users who require more advanced functionality, the paid tier is likely a better option. Ultimately, whether the free tier is "good enough" depends on the individual user's needs and expectations.
