Kling AI Pricing 2026

Kling AI Pricing in 2026: A Comprehensive Guide

Kling AI is an artificial intelligence chatbot platform designed to help businesses automate customer support, sales, and other operational tasks. With its advanced natural language processing (NLP) capabilities, Kling AI aims to improve conversation flows and provide more personalized experiences.

Pricing Tiers

Kling AI offers four pricing tiers to cater to different business needs:

  • Free

    Up to 1,000 conversations per month, basic analytics, and limited integrations.

  • Basic

    Up to 10,000 conversations per month, advanced analytics, and standard integrations. ($49/month)

  • Pro

    Up to 50,000 conversations per month, AI-driven automation, and enterprise integrations. ($199/month)

  • Enterprise

    Customized plans for large businesses, with dedicated support, advanced security, and custom integrations. (Contact for quote)

Best Plan for Most Users

The Pro plan is likely the best option for most businesses, offering a balance of advanced features and affordability. This tier provides the necessary tools for businesses to automate and optimize their customer support and sales operations.

Comparison Note vs Alternatives

Compared to its competitors, Kling AI offers a unique blend of advanced NLP capabilities and customization options.
